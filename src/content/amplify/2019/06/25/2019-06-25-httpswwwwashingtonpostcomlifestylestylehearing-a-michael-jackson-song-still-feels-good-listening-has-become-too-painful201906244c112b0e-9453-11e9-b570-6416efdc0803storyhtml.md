<p>You’re not listening to him anymore, but you still hear him. At nightclubs, restaurants, weddings, shopping malls, grocery stores, ballgames, house parties, block parties, cookouts, spin class, the pool, the dentist, the gym. Not in <i>your </i>headphones, though. Not in <i>your </i>car, not in <i>your </i>house. Just anywhere and everywhere else.</p>
