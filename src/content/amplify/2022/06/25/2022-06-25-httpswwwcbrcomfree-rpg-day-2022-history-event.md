<p>Every year in June, the Free RPG Day organization Gaming Days joins with forces with top RPG publishers worldwide to celebrate local game stores and the <a href="http://cbr.com/tag/tabletop/">tabletop RPGs</a> that continue to drive gamers through their doors. On June 25, Free RPG Day will take place in participating hobby game stores all across North America, with similar events taking place on July 23 outside the U.S. -- and there will be plenty of exciting activities and free games for everyone.</p>
<p>A number of participating publishers will be offering free RPGs, previews of upcoming games and merchandise and fun accessories to create a memorable event for gamers across a multitude of genres. Game stores may even have table setups for players to test micro RPGs and one-shots as the event takes place. Here's what Free RPG Day is, how it got started, and (most importantly) what can gamers expect from this year's event.</p>
<h2> The History of Free RPG Day </h2>
<figure><picture> <source data-srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=825&amp;dpr=1.5" media="(min-width: 1024px)" srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=825&amp;dpr=1.5"/> <source data-srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=825&amp;dpr=1.5" media="(min-width: 768px)" srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=825&amp;dpr=1.5"/> <source data-srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=800&amp;dpr=1.5" media="(min-width: 481px)" srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=800&amp;dpr=1.5"/> <source data-srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=500&amp;dpr=1.5" media="(min-width: 0px)" srcset="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg?q=50&amp;fit=crop&amp;w=500&amp;dpr=1.5"/> <img alt="A group of characters play a tabletop rpg" data-img-url="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg" src="https://static1.cbrimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/RPG-group-2.jpg"/> </picture><figcaption>A group of characters play a tabletop rpg</figcaption></figure>
<p>In 2019, four gaming retailers joined forces to create an exciting event for tabletop gamers as a thank you for their patronage over the years. They wanted to create annual events that would not only draw gamers to their stores, but celebrate the plethora of fun tabletop games the industry continues to produce. Unlike video games, tabletop doesn't always get the hype and recognition it deserves, and these fans and retailers wanted to change that with an event akin to the popular <a href="https://www.cbr.com/five-must-read-free-comic-book-day-2022-titles/">Free Comic Book Day</a>.</p>
<p>Free RPG Day was born as a way to not only appeal to gamers already invested in the tabletop world, but potentially draw in new players looking to meet like-minded gamers to share their interests and possibly even discover new ones. Gathering support from top RPG publishers, Gaming Days went on to establish two annual events designed to provide exposure for gaming stores and the publishers that fill their shelves with tabletop wonder, adventure, and excitement.</p>

<p>There are currently 17 game publishers involved in Free RPG 2022. Modiphius, <a href="https://www.cbr.com/tag/critical-role/"><em>Critical Role</em></a>'s Darrington Press, Magpie Games, and 9th Level are just a few of the game publishers providing micro adventures. Paizo will also contribute adventures for both <a href="https://www.cbr.com/pathfinder-2nd-edition-adventure-paths-ranked/"><em>Starfinder</em> and <em>Pathfinder</em></a>, while creators like WizKids, Dave Taylor Miniatures, and Loke Battle Mats will also be adding excitement to the event.</p>
<p>Sponsors for this year's Free RPG Day have yet to be announced, but interested gamers can <a href="https://www.freerpgday.com/">check out the official site</a> for updates from contributors, including sneak peeks at what they'll be offering on June 25. The site also offers a search bar to find out where participating game stores across the U.S. can be found.</p>
