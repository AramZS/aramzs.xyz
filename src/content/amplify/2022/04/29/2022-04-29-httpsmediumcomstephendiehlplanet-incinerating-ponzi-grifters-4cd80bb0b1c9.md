<p>Hidden beneath the techno-obscurantist story of allegedly purely digital money is a very physical smoldering train wreck of an inefficient technology that is utterly unfit for any purpose and whose energy consumption and environmental externalities are inexcusably horrific. Imagine if we had constructed a Las Vegas casino with the carbon footprint of Argentina, a country of 45 million people, all so that a few gamblers can get their next fix.</p>
<p>The inescapable problem at the heart of bitcoin technology is its inexcusable choice to use an algorithm known as Proof of Work. Simply put, the algorithm incentivizes network participants to waste energy by performing repeated SHA-256 hashes on random inputs attempting to find a solution to an artificially constructed problem as a means to build a “fair” global sortition function which acts as a means to run a lottery to confirm blocks of transactions in the network. All this aimless techno-solutionism amounts to is a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Queen%27s_race">Red Queen’s race</a> in which participants aimlessly waste compute cycles, and thus energy, to create an adversarial environment in which future participants are encouraged to waste even more energy to keep up. The algorithm is designed to waste energy and see who can increasingly waste more energy, extended into the future…forever.</p>
