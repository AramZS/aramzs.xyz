<p>If you saw Megan Thee Stallion at the 2022 Billboard Music Awards, then you also saw Cara Delevingne. The model and <a href="https://www.thecut.com/2021/06/cara-delevingne-tours-ad-around-her-adult-playhouse.html">“adult playhouse”</a> resident did not attend the award show with the rapper, at least in the conventional sense. However, anywhere Megan Thee Stallion went, Cara Delevingne was there, too.</p>

<p>While Megan posed on the red carpet, <a href="https://twitter.com/enews/status/1525998213265760256?s=20&amp;t=6rIun4mTJ40yw5FrbHVYaw">Delevingne was peering</a> from behind the step and repeat, mouthing what looks like the words “Yes, bitch.” Then, <a href="https://twitter.com/PopCrave/status/1526010090792402945?s=20&amp;t=udJKy8iNnVH2E7AqLUL5rg">Delevingne joined Megan on the red carpet</a>, tossing her train in the air for photos. Inside the awards show, Delevingne was inexplicably seated next to Megan and behind Doja Cat. When <a href="https://twitter.com/ruespillbottle/status/1526018677111500801?s=20&amp;t=gbtOFMnPHZBf2hSphV2CRQ">Megan won Top Rap Female Artist</a>, Delevingne celebrated with an obligatory tongue in Megan’s face. At one point, <a href="https://twitter.com/PopCrave/status/1526020919424057344?s=20&amp;t=-_4niZhZj3jwCOio2H6uoQ">Doja Cat was seen talking to Megan</a>, and suddenly, a rogue Delevingne leaned into the conversation to tell Doja Cat that she loves her. “Oh my God. Hi, Cara Delevingne,” Doja Cat responded.” Oh my god, hi, indeed.</p>
