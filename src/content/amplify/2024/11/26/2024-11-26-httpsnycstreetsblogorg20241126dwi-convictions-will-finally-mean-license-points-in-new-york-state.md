title: DWI Convictions Will Finally Mean License Points in New York State

It's DW-about time. The state Department of Motor Vehicles will finally add points to a driver's license after a conviction for intoxicated driving or aggravated unlicensed operation — both of which will receive 11 points under new guidelines that quietly went into effect earlier this month.
