<p>Gov. Andy Beshear of Kentucky <a href="https://www.kentucky.gov/Pages/Activity-stream.aspx?n=GovernorBeshear&amp;prId=2782">declared a state of emergency</a> on Saturday as heavy rain flooded parts of the state, leading to water rescues and reports of fatalities that officials were working to confirm.</p>
<p>“We’ve sadly already received reports of fatalities that we are working to confirm, and we need everyone to stay alert and do what’s needed to keep each other safe,” Mr. Beshear said.</p>
<p>Forecasters expect as much as seven inches of rain, he added.</p>
