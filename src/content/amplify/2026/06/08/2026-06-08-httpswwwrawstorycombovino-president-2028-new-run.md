<p>The man <a href="https://www.rawstory.com/tag/california">California</a> Gov. <a href="https://www.rawstory.com/tag/gavin-newsom">Gavin Newsom</a> (D) called "Gestapo Greg" is now gunning for the <a href="https://www.rawstory.com/tag/white-house">White House</a> — and he's ready to take on whoever President Donald <a href="https://www.rawstory.com/trump-news/">Trump</a> has in mind as his successor.</p>
<p>Gregory Bovino, the firebrand former Border Patrol commander-at-large who became the face of Trump's brutal immigration crackdowns in Los Angeles, Chicago, and Minneapolis, has <a href="https://www.newsnationnow.com/politics/bovino-exploring-white-house/">launched an exploratory committee</a> for a 2028 presidential bid, NewsNation confirmed Monday. The move puts him on a direct collision course with Trump, who has <a href="https://time.com/article/2026/06/03/trump-vance-rubio-2028-presidential-election/">publicly backed</a> a Vance-Rubio ticket as his preferred succession plan.</p>
<p>"If I were President, I'd lead that [deportation] effort from the front and be on the front lines from time to time," Bovino said in a statement <a href="https://www.thedailybeast.com/border-patrol-commander-greg-bovino-eyes-the-white-house/">first reported by the Daily Beast</a> — a barely veiled shot at Trump, whom he has accused of going soft on immigration.</p>
<p>The bid comes loaded with baggage. Bovino was <a href="https://www.newsnationnow.com/politics/bovino-exploring-white-house/">ousted in January</a> after federal agents under his command fatally shot two U.S. citizens in Minneapolis. He was widely photographed in a long dark coat that Newsom, speaking at the World Economic Forum in Davos, <a href="https://thehill.com/homenews/administration/5703060-gregory-bovino-gavin-newsom-trench-coat/">described</a> as looking like Bovino "literally went on eBay and purchased SS garb." When Bovino was subsequently removed from his post, Newsom wrote on X: "Gestapo Greg is out. Keep the pressure up. It's working."</p>
<p>More recently, Bovino <a href="https://www.lgbtqnation.com/2026/06/quick-hit-trumps-border-chief-speaks-alongside-neo-at-pro-summit/">attended a far-right "Remigration Summit"</a> in Portugal, where he was photographed alongside the event's organizer — a man who invoked the Weimar Republic as a model for mass deportation policy.</p>
<p>His own party isn't impressed. When asked about Bovino's criticisms of the Department of Homeland Security, DHS Secretary Markwayne Mullin delivered a withering dismissal: <a href="https://www.rawstory.com/mullin-bovino-irrelevant/">"I never met the guy. He's irrelevant to me. I don't know who he is."</a></p>
<p>Bovino's campaign website, Bovino2028.com, carries the slogan "House Bovino — Men Fight Back" and refers to immigrants as "foreign hordes."</p>
