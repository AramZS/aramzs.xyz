<h2>How TCG Curator Started</h2>
<p>I built this site because I wanted to find Pokémon cards by what’s actually in the art. The cards I personally collect are ones with Trainers and Pokémon cooking or eating food. But those are impossible to look up with existing search tools because they focus on card name, type, and rarity. I noticed plenty of other Pokémon collectors running into the same issues, each trying to curate their own collection.</p>
<p>I also wanted browsing to feel more fun and interactive than scrolling a database. That’s where the Curator came in — generate, rearrange, filter, generate again until you create the perfect binder page. Tags are community-built so the more that people contribute, the easier it gets for everyone to find their new favorites!</p>
