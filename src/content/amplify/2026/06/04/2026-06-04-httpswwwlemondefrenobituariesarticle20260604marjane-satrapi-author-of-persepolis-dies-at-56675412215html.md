<p><img alt="Marjane Satrapi in Paris, in 2009." src="https://img.lemde.fr/2026/06/04/0/0/4445/4416/664/0/75/0/e8beb0c_upload-1-rvkpo6qo2kgk-satrapi-marjane3.jpg"/>Marjane Satrapi in Paris, in 2009.  FRANCK COURTÈS/VU FOR LE MONDE</p>
<p>The French-Iranian artist Marjane Satrapi, who became known worldwide through the graphic novel and film <em>Persepolis</em>, has died at the age of 56, people close to her told Agence France-Presse on Thursday, June 4.</p>
<p>"Marjane Satrapi died of sadness a little over a year after the death of Mattias Ripa, her husband and the love of her life," they said in a statement sent to AFP.</p>
<p><em>This is a developing story.</em></p>
