<h2><p>Democrat Ro Khanna cited a 2025 study that estimated more than 14 million people could die without USAID resources by 2030</p></h2>
<p>Monday 22 June 2026 21:55 BST</p>
<figure><p class="rw-outer-content"><span>Some content could not be imported from the original document.</span> <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/elon-musk-ro-khanna-doge-cuts-b3000732.html">View content ↗ </a></p><figcaption>Democrat Rep. Ro Khanna says Elon Musk 'possibly sentenced to death' 4.5 million children by cutting USAID</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/elon-musk-trump-doge-chainsaw-cpac-b2900410.html">Elon Musk, the trillionaire CEO</a> and former temporary government employee, threatened to sue Democratic Rep. Ro Khanna after the lawmaker accused Musk of “possibly” sentencing 4.5 million children to death by <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/usaid-close-trump-administration-b2723617.html">cutting funding to the U.S. Agency for International Development. </a></p>
<p>Monday morning, Musk took to his social media platform, X, to lash out at Khanna for suggesting <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/usaid-trump-food-aid-waste-b2800939.html">Musk’s deep cuts to USAID’s funding </a>and workforce, while overseeing DOGE last year, may have led to millions of children dying.</p>
<p>“You know they’re celebrating that he created 4,400 millionaires, but they don’t talk about the 4.5 million children around the world who he possibly sentenced to death by dismantling <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/climate-change/usaid-trump-climate-aid-africa-doge-musk-b2729392.html">USAID</a>,” Khanna said on the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U6orysyO7Ww">“I’ve Had It” podcast</a> over the weekend.</p>
<p>Musk responded by calling the progressive Democrat a “<a href="https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2069083221300576664">liar</a>” and “<a href="https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2069089477511790812">robber</a>” while also <a href="https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2069087335824699744">accusing him of insider stock trading.</a></p>
<p>“Time to sue this liar,” <a href="https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2069083221300576664">Musk wrote</a>.</p>

<p><em>The Independent</em> has asked Khanna’s office for comment.</p>
<p>Musk, <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/trump-doge-social-security-voter-fraud-b2904343.html">who temporarily oversaw DOGE,</a> a Trump administration initiative to scale down the federal workforce and make massive cuts to what he called wasteful government spending, has been defensive of the group’s cuts.</p>
<p>Monday, he insisted DOGE’s cuts to USAID were the result of a new rule that required the agency to contact aid recipients to confirm funds were being used legitimately. He pointed to a <a href="https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/usaid-official-and-three-corporate-executives-plead-guilty-decade-long-bribery-scheme">Justice Department release</a> that said one USAID employee and three contractors pleaded guilty to bribery and fraud.</p>
<p>“The reality is that money was being sent to corrupt politicians under the guise of aid! Liars and stock insider traders like Ro the Robber should be in prison!!”<a href="https://x.com/elonmusk/status/2069087335824699744"> Musk wrote. </a></p>
<p>Khanna <a href="https://x.com/RoKhanna/status/2069043719928418580">responded</a> to Musk’s attacks by pointing to a <a href="https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736(25)01186-9/fulltext">2025 study</a> that determined, without access to USAID resources and funding, 14 million people in low-and-middle-income countries could die by 2030 – including more than 4.5 million children under the age of 5.</p>
<figure><img alt="Khanna, a progressive Democrat, said Musk should be investigated for making massive cuts to USAID – which was effectively shuttered by the administration" src="https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/06/22/20/2246746935..?quality=75&amp;width=640&amp;auto=webp" srcset="https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/06/22/20/2246746935..?quality=75&amp;width=320&amp;auto=webp 320w, https://static.independent.co.uk/2026/06/22/20/2246746935..?quality=75&amp;width=640&amp;auto=webp 640w"/><figcaption>Khanna, a progressive Democrat, said Musk should be investigated for making massive cuts to USAID – which was effectively shuttered by the administration (AFP/Getty)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Advocates of USAID had long warned of the potential consequences of shuttering USAID, which assisted in researching and providing medical solutions to global health problems such as HIV/AIDS, malaria, polio and more. The agency also assisted in emergency disaster relief, providing nutrition to areas experiencing famine, bolstering economic development and more.</p>
<p>USAID, once the world’s largest foreign aid agency, was effectively closed down by the Trump administration last year.</p>
<p>Officials had claimed the agency was wasteful by promoting baseless theories about USAID’s funding on social media, including that it funded a transgender opera in Colombia – <a href="https://www.factcheck.org/2025/02/sorting-out-the-facts-on-waste-and-abuse-at-usaid/">which was false.</a> Musk himself called USAID a <a href="https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/elon-musk-usaid-trump-doge-b2691123.html">“criminal organization”</a> and insisted it needed to “die.”</p>
<p>On the “I’ve Had It” podcast, Khanna said Musk needed “to answer” for cuts to USAID.</p>
<p>“He needs to be subpoenaed. He needs to face investigation. He needs to answer for what he did with DOGE,” Khanna added.</p>
