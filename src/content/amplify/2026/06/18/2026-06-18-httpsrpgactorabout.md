<p>The universal RPG character registry, built on your own data</p>
<h2>So... What is this?</h2>
<p><strong>rpg.actor</strong> is a universal character registry for roleplaying games. It lets you store your persona's stats and sprite in your own <strong>Atmosphere Account</strong>, so you can log in to <em>any</em> compatible game, system, or experience as your persistent character!</p>
<h2>Alright... What can I do?</h2>
<h2>Hold on... How does this work?</h2>

<h3>Your Data, Your Way</h3>
<p><strong>rpg.actor</strong> stores your character data as portable records directly in your own personal data server, meaning no one but you can approve changes. Sprites and stats live under the <code>actor.rpg.*</code> namespace in your account — fully portable, readable by any compatible app, and explorable across the open social web!</p>
