<h2>A trove of <em>Daggerheart</em> treasures</h2>
<p><em>Hope Springs Eternal </em>offers 125 pages of original content made for the <a href="https://tabletopbookshelf.com/products/daggerheart-core-set"><em>Daggerheart TTRPG</em></a>, not a conversion of existing material. Each of its 5 campaign frames is complemented by a location usable in any campaign (locations can include landmarks, settlements, NPCs, factions, rumors, adversaries, environments, loot).</p>
<p>Will you brave the Ever Rime and shepherd your Wonderbeast throughout Fairreach, help Fermata's communities in order to restore the Eternal Song, embark on a Gutterpunk Rhapsody and tear down the dystopian Tower, lead a broken hero to the Jagged Domain before their Final Curtain, or help the Gardens get rid of its Poisoned Roots?</p>
<p><em>125 pages, 9 x 6 inches, full-color softcover RPG Book</em></p>
