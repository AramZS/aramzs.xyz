<p>Microsoft has refuted claims that its rival system, the PlayStation 5, is outselling the Xbox edition of <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/gta-6-everything-we-know-so-far-9357">Grand Theft Auto 6</a>, claiming that the figure "doesn't represent pre-order data" and insisting "we've had record orders".</p>
<p>Yesterday (27th June), IGN tweeted that according to its community, <a href="https://x.com/IGN/status/2070671284481179813">players were eight times more likely to have pre-ordered the game on PS5 than on Xbox Series X/S</a>.</p>
<p>In a response to <a href="https://www.windowscentral.com/gaming/xbox/this-doesnt-represent-pre-order-data-xbox-disputes-reports-of-ps5-crushing-gta-6-preorder-demand">Windows Central</a>, however, Microsoft hit back at the claim, insisting the data collated via IGN's Finds' and commerce affiliate linking program was not representative and suggesting we wait for "real data".</p>

<p>“This doesn't represent pre-order data. We've had record orders. People should wait for real data and not clicks on affiliate links," Microsoft said in a statement.</p>
<p>It's been a busy week for GTA 6-related news. Pre-orders went live in the week and <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/gta-6-prices-confirmed">we finally got a price, too</a>. But there was outrage around reports there will be <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/grand-theft-auto-6-preorders-gta6-no-disc">no disc in the physical edition</a>, only a download code, <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/grand-theft-auto-6-physical-no-disc-experts">a decision many think hints at wider plans to crush second-hand sales</a> and prompting <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/gta-6-exciting-but-we-need-to-talk-about-game-owenership-fast">a debate about game ownership</a>. We also got a batch of new screenshots detailing exclusive Ultimate Edition shops, missions, cars, and outfits in the game.</p>
<p>Yesterday, another new report cast doubt on hopes that a physical version of Grand Theft Auto 6 will follow the digital release later this year. While some insisted that <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/thats-not-the-plan-take-two-ceo-shoots-down-rumors-of-no-physical-gta-6-copies-at-launch">a proper physical edition with a disc would launch in December,</a> The Hollywood Reporter sources insisted <a href="https://www.eurogamer.net/gta-6-physical-disc-in-december-insider-claims">this isn't true</a>, stressing that "the language [from a Rockstar email] is being misinterpreted by a hopeful internet".</p>
