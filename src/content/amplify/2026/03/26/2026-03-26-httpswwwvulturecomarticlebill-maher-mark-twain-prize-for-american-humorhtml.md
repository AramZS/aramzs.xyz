<p>The great Norm Macdonald once referred to <a href="https://x.com/seanoconnz/status/1699586205577609313">Bill Maher</a> as “maybe the unfunniest person I’ve ever encountered that’s called a comedian.” Completely unrelated to that: The White House just announced that Maher will be the first recipient of the Mark Twain Prize for American Humor under the newly appointed management of the newly renamed Trump Kennedy Center. Great news for your divorced uncle who thinks “socially liberal, but fiscally conservative” is a reasonable worldview. The news arrives just days after the White House dismissed <a href="https://www.theatlantic.com/culture/2026/03/trump-bill-maher-kennedy-center/686474/">early reports of Maher’s selection</a> as “fake news.” The <em>Real Time</em> host is an inspired selection in that there are few other comedians who so routinely anger people on both sides of the political spectrum. Credit where it’s due: Trump isn’t always known for his bipartisanship.</p>
<p>Joking aside, the selection does come as a bit of a surprise considering Trump slammed Maher as a “highly overrated LIGHTWEIGHT” on <a href="https://variety.com/2026/tv/news/trump-bill-maher-white-house-dinner-waste-of-time-1236663505/">Truth Social</a> just last month. In his post, Trump called Maher out for apparently changing his tune after the pair sat down for dinner last year, after which Maher called him “gracious and measured” on his show. It’s doubly surprising as the White House recently announced plans to shut down operations of the Kennedy Center for two years and called the future of the Mark Twain Prize into uncertainty. Seemingly, they could not resist the opportunity to honor a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vln9D81eO60">virulent Islamophobe</a> on their way out the door. In a statement released alongside the announcement, Maher expressed his gratitude and joked that it is “humbling to get anything named for a man who’s been thrown out of as many school libraries as Mark Twain.”</p>
<p>Maher will be presented with the award on June 28; the ceremony will be taped for a special to air on Netflix.
