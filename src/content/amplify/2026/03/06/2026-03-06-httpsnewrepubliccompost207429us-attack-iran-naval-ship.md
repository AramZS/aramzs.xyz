<p>The U.S. Navy’s attack on an Iranian frigate, the IRIS <i>Dena</i><i>,</i> on Wednesday was the first time an American submarine has sunk an enemy ship since World War II. But the <i>Dena</i> may not have been armed because it was <a href="https://apnews.com/article/iran-warship-iris-dena-india-14916ad657e50f048bbeb42b38224ecb">returning</a> from an international exercise in the Indian Ocean, and the U.S. Navy likely knew it because it was taking part in the same exercise.</p>
<p>Both the United States and Iran were taking part in the MILAN 2026 exercise, organized by the Indian Navy, on February 15–26, with the U.S. <a href="https://www.navy.mil/Press-Office/News-Stories/display-news/Article/4417628/us-navy-concludes-participation-in-multilateral-exercise-milan-2026/">sending</a> a maritime patrol aircraft and Iran sending the <i>Dena.</i> Iranian sailors from the ship <a href="https://x.com/kmmishratv/status/2029196076478316781">paraded</a> on land before India’s president.</p>

<p>The exercise in question required ships not to carry any ammunition. Normally, the <i>Dena</i> <a href="http://www.hisutton.com/Iranian-Navy-Mowj-Class.html">carries</a> various missiles and guns, including anti-ship missiles. Because the U.S. also took part, it would have been aware that the <i>Dena</i> was unarmed. Former Indian Foreign Minister Kanwal Sibal <a href="https://x.com/KanwalSibal/status/2029438199546954240">accused</a> the attack of being “premeditated as the US was aware of the Iranian ship’s presence in the exercise.”</p>
<p>“The US has ignored India’s sensitivities as the ship was in these waters because of India’s invitation,” Sibal said in a post on X. The Iranian ambassador to India, Mohammad Fathali, <a href="https://www.thestatesman.com/india/execution-at-sea-was-iris-dena-iranian-frigate-sunk-by-us-in-the-indian-ocean-unarmed-1503566523.html">condemned</a> the attack and said the ship was unarmed.</p>
<p>“We will respond to this assassination very strongly. This ship was unarmed and in a regular maneuver at sea. I think that the United States and the Zionist regime want to disturb and destroy all the international law and international norms,” Fathali said.</p>
<p>At least 87 sailors were <a href="https://www.yahoo.com/news/articles/least-87-dead-us-sinks-151847524.html">killed</a> in the torpedo attack in international waters in the Indian Ocean, and the Sri Lankan navy responded to the <i>Dena</i>’s distress call and rescued 32 survivors, but 61 members of the crew are still missing. The U.S. didn’t respond to the call, and Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth crowed about the attack to reporters on Wednesday.</p>
<p>“An American submarine sunk an Iranian warship that thought it was safe in international waters. Instead, it was sunk by a torpedo,” Hegseth said, calling it a “quiet death.” As a result of the attack, an Iranian supply tanker that was also near Sri Lanka, the IRIS <i>Bushehr,</i> has taken <a href="https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/world/south-asia/sri-lanka-evacuates-over-200-crew-members-of-2nd-iranian-vessel-day-after-us-sunk-iris-dena/articleshow/129103444.cms">refuge</a> in the island country.</p>
<p>The sinking of the <i>Dena</i> seems to be part of Hegseth’s new policy of <a href="https://newrepublic.com/post/207334/hegseth-playing-keeps-iran">ignoring</a> “stupid rules of engagement” and “punching them while they’re down,” and it raises questions of what the Trump administration would say if a country like Iran attacked an American ship it knew was unarmed.</p>
