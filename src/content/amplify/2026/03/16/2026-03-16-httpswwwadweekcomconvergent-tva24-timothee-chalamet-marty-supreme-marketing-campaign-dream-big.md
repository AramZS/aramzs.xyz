<p>A24’s Marty Supreme is living up to its namesake, proving to be one of the most eagerly anticipated films of the year.</p>
<p>According to <a href="https://deadline.com/2025/12/indie-film-box-office-marty-supreme-timothee-chalamet-1236654607/">Deadline</a>, the film has already achieved the fastest-selling presale for an A24 movie and the biggest per-theater average for a limited release in 2025, grossing $875,000 from 6 theaters in one weekend.</p>
<p>Loosely based on <a href="https://www.smithsonianmag.com/history/how-marty-reisman-the-real-life-inspiration-behind-marty-supreme-revolutionized-the-sport-of-table-tennis-180987879/">real-life table tennis player Marty Reisman</a>, Marty Supreme, which will be released in the U.S. on Christmas Day, follows a fictional ping-pong prodigy’s rise through the underground table tennis scene in 1950s New York City. However, through the film’s innovative marketing, Marty Supreme has become less of a character in a movie and more of a star in reality.</p>
