<p><strong>A DEAD PLANET, A FORSAKEN KNIGHT AND A GROUP OF STRANDED SPACEFARERS</strong></p>
<p><em>Earth is dead, once radiant with heroes and villains, gods and monsters, myth and magic. Then the Horror from Beyond fell upon it, a baleful thing of void-born malice. Now ghouls prowl the blasted wastes and decaying ruins, seeking their absent master.</em></p>
<p><em>The Knight Errant alone defied that cosmic pestilence. He shackled the Horror within his own flesh, becoming both prison and prisoner. Centuries later he endures, frozen in his sanctum, a sentinel damned to eternity.</em></p>
<p><em>Far across the gulfs of space, the scattered progeny of Earth raised civilizations of metal and science, trading magic for machines. Yet the old world lingers in legend, attracting spacefarers and outcasts. But a few, burdened by mischance, are cast down upon this necrotic sphere, stranded in the shadow of forgotten ruin, where only peril promises reward.<br/>

<p><em><strong>The Knight Errant: Expanded Edition</strong></em> is a science fantasy old school Tabletop RPG adventure, designed for any Mark of the Odd games (<em>Into the Odd, Electric Bastionland, Cairn, Eco Mofos!!</em>), inspired by the works of Weird Fiction authors such as Clark Ashton Smith, H. P. Lovecraft, Jack Vance, and Michael Moorcock.</p>
<p>Featuring:</p>
<ul><li>A necrotic wasteland with five locations and a nine room dungeon for a short campaign, drawn in isometric style! </li><li> Illustrations for the NPCs and creatures that the players will encounter! </li><li> <em>Portents</em> for each location, inspired by <em>Omens</em> from <em>Mythic Bastionland</em> and Corpo schemes from <em>Eco Mofos!! Islands of Weirdhope</em>. The locations have their own story to tell! </li><li> Three factions right out of the Weird Fiction genre. Cultists, Robot Soldiers, and Wasteland Survivors! </li><li> Random Encounters Tables and Random Treasures Tables to support your GMing needs!</li><li>PDFs, colored and black &amp; white, single and spread<br/> </li><li>Zip file containing the illustrations and maps </li></ul>
