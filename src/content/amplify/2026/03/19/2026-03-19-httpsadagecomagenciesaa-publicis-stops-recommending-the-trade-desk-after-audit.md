<p>Publicis Groupe is telling clients it will no longer recommend The Trade Desk as a demand-side platform for digital media buying following a recently completed audit. The holding company is telling clients that the audit it commissioned revealed multiple violations of the master service agreement it has in place with The Trade Desk.</p>
