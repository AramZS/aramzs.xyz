<p>Arguably one of the most famous satirical publications, <a href="https://theonion.com/"><em>The Onion</em></a>, has made a triumphant return to its roots, returning to publishing a monthly printed newspaper years after it was taken to an online-only format. Senior editor Toni McQuilken had the chance to sit down with Danielle Strle, chief product officer; and Jimmy Hasse, creative director of editorial art, <em>The Onion</em>; as well as Andy Sanchez, president of <a href="https://www.topweb.net/">Topweb</a>, the Chicago, Illinois-based commercial printer who took on the actual production of the revitalized print edition. Check out the video to learn more about why <em>The Onion</em> has returned to print, the challenges they faced to get there, and where they're going next.</p>
