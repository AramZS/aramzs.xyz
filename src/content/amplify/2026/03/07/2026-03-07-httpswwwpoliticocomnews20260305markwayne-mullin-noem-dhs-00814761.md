<p>President Donald Trump on Thursday said he would name Sen. Markwayne Mullin (R-Okla.) to be the next Homeland Security secretary, moving current DHS chief Kristi Noem to a special envoy role around Western Hemisphere cooperation <a data-tracking="mpos=center&amp;mid=ar_body&amp;lindex=61&amp;lcol=" href="https://www.politico.com/news/2026/03/05/pressure-mounts-on-trump-to-fire-noem-00814427"><u>in the face of growing frustrations with her tenure</u></a>.</p>
<p>In a Truth Social post, Trump said Noem “has served us well, and has had numerous and spectacular results (especially on the Border!).” Trump added Noem will become a special envoy for the “our new Security Initiative in the Western Hemisphere we are announcing on Saturday” at a summit with Latin American leaders on Saturday in Florida.</p>
<p>“I thank Kristi for her service at “Homeland,” he continued.</p>
