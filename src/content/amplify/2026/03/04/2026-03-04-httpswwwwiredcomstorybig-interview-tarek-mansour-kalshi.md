<p>If you picked Seattle to win <a href="https://www.wired.com/story/super-bowl-lx-tailgate-photo-essay/">Super Bowl LX</a>, there were multiple means to turn your hunch into cash. You could place a wager on a betting site like FanDuel or DraftKings. Or you might risk the same amount of money by taking a position in the <a href="https://www.wired.com/tag/prediction-markets/">prediction market</a> known as <a href="https://www.wired.com/story/iranian-ayatollah-khameneis-death-sparks-revolt-among-kalshi-customers/">Kalshi</a>. In both cases you would have been rewarded.</p>
