<h1><strong>How it works</strong></h1>
<p>Times New Resistance autocorrects specific words as they are typed. For example, the word <em>ICE</em> autocorrects to <em>the</em> <em>Goon Squad </em>and the word <em>Trump</em> autocorrects to <em>Donald Trump is a felon.</em></p>
<p>To the untrained eye, Times New Resistance looks just like Times New Roman – <a href="https://www.npr.org/2025/12/11/nx-s1-5639915/u-s-state-department-changes-official-font-from-calibri-back-to-times-new-roman">the official font of the U.S. State Department</a>. When you install the font, <strong>it will appear in your font menus as Times New Roman</strong>, with an extra space between the words Times and New.</p>
