<p>As Adobe <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2025/10/28/adobe-launches-ai-assistants-for-express-and-photoshop/">ramps</a> <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/10/adobe-brings-photoshop-express-and-acrobat-features-to-chatgpt/">up</a> <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2024/04/23/adobe-claims-its-new-image-generation-model-is-its-best-yet/">its</a> <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2025/02/12/adobe-launches-firefly-ai-subscriptions/">investments</a> in <a href="https://techcrunch.com/2025/10/20/adobe-launches-a-foundry-service-that-builds-custom-generative-ai-models-for-enterprises/">AI</a>, the company has decided to shut down its 2D animation software, <a href="https://www.adobe.com/products/animate.html">Adobe Animate</a>. On Monday, Adobe issued an <a href="https://helpx.adobe.com/animate/release-notes.html">update</a> to the company‚Äôs support site and sent emails to existing customers announcing Adobe Animate will be discontinued on March 1, 2026.</p>
<p>Enterprise customers can continue to receive technical support through March 1, 2029, to ease the transition. Other customers will have support through March of next year, the company said.</p>
<p>The decision has been <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/mildlyinfuriating/comments/1qu41ca/adobe_is_planning_to_discontinue_animate_next/">met</a> <a href="https://x.com/search?q=adobe%20animate&amp;src=typed_query&amp;f=live">with</a> incredulity, disappointment, and anger among Adobe Animate users, who are concerned about the lack of alternatives that mirror Animate‚Äôs functionality.</p>
<p>One customer, <a href="https://x.com/TylerGlaiel/status/2018394595340771653">posting on X</a>, pleaded with Adobe to at least open source the software rather than abandon it. Commenters on the thread responded with angst, saying things <a href="https://x.com/ThisIsWronchi/status/2018404802510630914">like</a> ‚Äúthis is legit gonna ruin my life,‚Äù <a href="https://x.com/Ari4472/status/2018406974589722819">and</a> ‚Äúliterally what the hell are they doing? animate is the reason a good chunk of adobe users even subscribe in the first place.</p>
<p>Adobe explained its decision to discontinue the program in an <a href="https://helpx.adobe.com/uk/animate/kb/end-of-life.html">FAQ</a>, saying, ‚ÄúAnimate has been a product that has existed for over 25 years and has served its purpose well for creating, nurturing, and developing the animation ecosystem. As technologies evolve, new platforms and paradigms emerge that better serve the needs of the users. Acknowledging this change, we are planning to discontinue supporting Animate.‚Äù</p>
