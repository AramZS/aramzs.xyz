<p>Most dietary supplements are not harmful. Some, however, should be taken with caution, especially in certain populations of people.</p>
<p>I specialize in treating people who are immunocompromised from leukemia or other <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/wellness/2025/11/12/surprising-cancer-symptoms/">cancers</a> affecting the cells of the bone marrow. Recently, I was reviewing the medications one of my patients was taking when she showed me a photo of a pill bottle with a label indicating that the contents included antioxidants.</p>
