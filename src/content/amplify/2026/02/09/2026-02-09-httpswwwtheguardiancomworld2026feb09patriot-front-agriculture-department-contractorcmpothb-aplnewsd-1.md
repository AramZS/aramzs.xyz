<h2 data-gu-name="standfirst">Ian Michael Elliott of neofascist Patriot Front worked ‘crisis relief missions’ funded by Department of Agriculture</h2>
<p>A federal security contractor that has been awarded millions of dollars by the Department of Agriculture hired a prominent white nationalist leader to work on its patrols last year.</p>
<p>Ian Michael Elliott, a longstanding senior figure in the neofascist group Patriot Front, was part of “crisis relief missions” undertaken on the US west coast by Knight Division Tactical, according to an image shared on LinkedIn in September by one of the company’s executives.</p>
<p>Elliott is shown posing alongside other people as he helps hold up a flag with the contractor’s logo on it. “Stellar company, sharp agents, quality work!” he would later write, in a five-star review posted on Knight Division Tactical’s Google Maps page.</p>

<p>Knight Division Tactical did not respond to multiple requests for comment, though the company executive deleted his LinkedIn post showing Elliott after the Guardian reached out.</p>
<p>Elliott has long been a senior figure in Patriot Front, which researchers have <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2023/03/09/us/politics/white-supremacist-propaganda.html">identified</a> as <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/world/2022/mar/03/white-supremacist-propaganda-anti-defamation-league?">responsible</a> for producing the majority of white supremacist propaganda in the US.</p>
<p>The group, which markets itself to disillusioned white men, has been <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2022/sep/02/patriot-front-recruits-members-young-pyramid-scheme">likened</a> to a “white nationalist pyramid scheme”. In 2023, five of its members were <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.nytimes.com/2023/07/21/us/patriot-front-idaho-pride-convicted.html">convicted</a> over a plot to start a riot at a gay pride event.</p>

<p>Patriot Front is perhaps best known for organizing <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.kcrg.com/2025/02/23/patriot-front-group-seen-marching-des-moines/">militaristic</a> <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.advocate.com/news/patriot-front-nashville-march">white</a> <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2023/jun/16/patriot-front-far-right-violence">nationalist</a> marches through US cities, with nearly all of the participants hiding their faces. After a March in Louisville, Kentucky, last year, Courier Journal columnist Joseph Gerth <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://eu.courier-journal.com/story/opinion/columnists/gerth/2025/07/08/louisville-neo-nazis-patriot-front-kentucky/84491564007/">called</a> them “masked cowards” who “despise anyone who believes in an inclusive society”.</p>
<p>Earlier this month, Patriot Front members were <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.reutersconnect.com/item/patriot-front-at-march-for-life/dGFnOnJldXRlcnMuY29tLDIwMjY6bmV3c21sX01UMU5VUlBITzAwMEIxT0ZHQQ">present</a> at the National Mall in Washington DC during the annual anti-abortion March for Life, where JD Vance spoke. Video posted by the group on Telegram shows that Elliott, who frequently serves as the bodyguard of the Patriot Front founder Thomas Rousseau, was among them.</p>
<p>Last year, Nashville’s NewsChannel 5 <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.newschannel5.com/news/newschannel-5-investigates/this-isnt-your-granddads-kkk-inside-the-influential-hate-group-thats-expanding-in-tennessee">revealed</a> that Elliott is one of the leaders of efforts by Patriot Front to develop a 122-acre (49-hectare) compound near Tellico Plains, Tennessee, where white nationalists receive combat training in preparation for what they believe is a coming race war.</p>
<p>“They’re kind of an entry portal, a place that seeks to recruit young, disillusioned white men who are interested in mixed martial arts, interested in fighting, interested in the idea of saving western civilization,” said Jon Lewis, a research fellow at the program on extremism at George Washington University who studies US white supremacist movements.</p>
<p>He added that Patriot Front is one of several groups that has contributed to “the mainstreaming of fringe concepts that have helped radicalize young men, like the <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2022/may/17/great-replacement-theory-explainer">‘great replacement’ theory</a>, something now echoed by figures close to the White House like Stephen Miller and Elon Musk”.</p>
<p>Elliott also leads <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/oct/17/russia-street-fight-club-far-right-active-club">Patria Gloria</a>, a white nationalist grappling team <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.counterextremism.com/press/extremist-content-online-isis-content-located-instagram-founders-base-and-active-club">affiliated</a> with Patriot Front, and the neo-Nazi active clubs movement ⁠–⁠ Telegram posts show that Patria Gloria invited several active clubs to participate in training at the Tellico Plains compound last spring.</p>
<p>Experts have <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.justsecurity.org/87970/amid-robert-rundos-extradition-the-white-supremacist-active-clubs-network-remains-a-threat/">warned</a> that active clubs make up a decentralized network of combat-ready white nationalist cells, and the Southern Poverty Law Center reported last year that Patriot Front <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.splcenter.org/resources/hatewatch/patriot-front-active-clubs-network-influence/">secretly controls</a> about a dozen of them; video posted on Patria Gloria’s Telegram channel shows that Elliott represented Patriot Front at a tournament hosted by the Southern California Active Club last year.</p>
<p>Recently, a Russian street fighting promoter, whose ties to US white nationalists and active clubs the Guardian <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/oct/17/russia-street-fight-club-far-right-active-club">revealed</a> last October, posted to Instagram that Elliott is joining its stable of fighters under the alias Norman.</p>
<p>Images posted on Telegram show Elliott training fellow white nationalists at Patriot Front’s Tennessee compound. CNN <a data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.cnn.com/2025/12/15/us/patriot-front-white-supremacists-tennessee">reported</a> last month that the building where Elliott held the training has been put up for sale, though Patriot Front intends to move forward with its plans to build out a large compound on surrounding land.</p>
<p>Elliott did not reply to a request for comment via Telegram. The app shows a message sent to his account was viewed.</p>
