<p>The vice president traveled to Budapest as Trump’s deadline for an Iran deal loomed Tuesday, backing the administration’s closest ideological ally in Europe.</p>
<p>BUDAPEST — As the hours ticked down to the Tuesday deadline his boss had set to escalate bombings in Iran, Vice President JD Vance’s day was consumed by another matter: helping the prime minister of Hungary as he faced his toughest election yet.</p>
