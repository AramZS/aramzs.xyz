<p><a href="https://pressgazette.co.uk/news/the-seo-parasites-buying-exploiting-and-ultimately-killing-online-newsbrands/">A Press Gazette investigation into parasite SEO firm Clickout Media </a>has been removed from Google’s search index after a spurious legal complaint.</p>
<p>On Wednesday (25 March), Press Gazette revealed how UK-based Clickout Media has bought a number of news websites in order to exploit their reputations in Google and promote online casinos.</p>
<p>In some cases, journalists have been fired and replaced with AI-powered writers. Some sites were removed from Google’s search results as a result of Press Gazette’s reporting, effectively killing the sites off.</p>
