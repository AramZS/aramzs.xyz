<p>A clip posted by an Instagram account bearing Abdulkarim’s name shows him lighting paper goods on fire in the warehouse while taking shots at his company.</p>
<p>“All you had to do was pay us enough to live,” he repeats in the video.</p>
<p>Before Abdulkarim allegedly torched the warehouse, he had complained about not being paid for lost break time while working as an hourly employee for an aviation services company, court documents show.</p>
<p>In 2024, he filed a class-action lawsuit against PrimeFlight, which provides “air carriers and airports with a wide range of aircraft, passenger and security services,” according to the company’s web site.</p>
