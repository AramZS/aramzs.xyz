<p>President <strong>Donald Trump</strong> went on a posting spree on Thursday after First Lady <strong>Melania Trump</strong> surprised everyone with an announcement about <strong>Jeffrey Epstein</strong>.</p>
<p>The first lady delivered remarks from the White House regarding Epstein, a former friend of her husband’s.</p>
<p>“I have never been friends with Epstein. Donald and I were invited to the same parties as Epstein from time to time, since overlapping in social circles is common in New York City and Palm Beach,” she <a href="https://www.mediaite.com/media/news/just-in-melania-trump-breaks-silence-on-epstein-vehemently-denies-any-ties-in-stunning-presser/">said</a> before denying any relationship with <strong>Ghislaine Maxwell</strong>, Epstein’s friend who is currently serving a 20-year sentence for sex trafficking. “To be clear, <a href="https://www.mediaite.com/media/piers-morgan-confronts-melania-director-brett-ratner-on-photo-with-epstein/">I never had a relationship</a> with Epstein or his accomplice, Maxwell.”</p>
<p>The first lady’s comments seemed to come out of nowhere, as the Epstein files have largely been out of the news cycle.</p>
<p>A few hours after Melania Trump’s comments, the president went on a Truth Social posting bender, beginning with a <a href="https://www.mediaite.com/politics/nut-jobs-trump-goes-scorched-earth-on-megyn-kelly-tucker-carlson-and-other-major-maga-critics-in-jaw-dropping-post/">lengthy screed</a> against MAGA influencers who have criticized his war in Iran. He singled out <strong>Tucker Carlson, Megyn Kelly</strong>,<strong> Candace Owens</strong>, and <strong>Alex Jones</strong>, calling them “stupid people” and “NUT JOBS.”</p>
<p>Next, Trump lashed out at Iran, which is still restricting traffic in the Strait of Hormuz because Israel has not paused its offensive in southern Lebanon.</p>
<p>“There are reports that Iran is charging fees to tankers going through the Hormuz Strait — They better not be and, if they are, they better stop now!” he wrote.</p>
<p>The president followed that by <a href="https://www.mediaite.com/politics/trump/trump-seethes-at-murdoch-owned-wall-street-journal-one-of-the-worst/">slamming</a> <em>The Wall Street Journal</em> Editorial Board for saying he took a “premature” victory lap after the ceasefire with Iran.</p>
<p>Trump followed this by giving an update on the reflecting pool on the National Mall.</p>
<p>Then came another warning to Iran.</p>
<p>The president then announced a <a href="https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116377358457034052">nomination</a> to the Sixth Circuit Court of Appeals and, in another post, <a href="https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116377366086619888">promoted</a> a book by Rep.<strong> Elise Stefanik</strong> (R-NY).</p>
<p>Subsequently, Trump reiterated his endorsement of Hungarian Prime Minister <strong>Viktor Orbán</strong> in the country’s election on Sunday.</p>
<p>Lastly, the president <a href="https://www.mediaite.com/media/news/trump-posts-raw-video-of-woman-being-murdered-with-hammer/">posted</a> a video of a convenience store clerk being beaten to death with a hammer.</p>
<p>The alleged assailant is an immigrant from Haiti who overstayed his temporary protected status, which expired in 2024, <a href="https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/04/07/haitian-illegal-alien-violently-kills-innocent-mother-repeatedly-hitting-her-hammer">according to</a> the Department of Homeland Security.</p>
