<h2> <a href="https://kottke.org/26/03/hypercard-changed-everything"> HyperCard Changed Everything</a></h2>
<figure><img alt="Play: Video" src="https://i.ytimg.com/vi_webp/hxHkNToXga8/maxresdefault.webp"/></figure>
<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hxHkNToXga8">This video</a> traces the history of Apple’s HyperCard from <a href="https://worrydream.com/refs/Bush%20-%20As%20We%20May%20Think%20(Life%20Magazine%209-10-1945).pdf">Vannevar Bush’s idea of the Memex</a> to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Mother_of_All_Demos">the Mother of All Demos</a> to <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v8VYeetOPuM">the Xerox PARC Alto</a> to <a href="https://apple.fandom.com/wiki/Bill_Atkinson">Bill Atkinson</a>, the inventor of HyperCard, who said:</p>
<blockquote><p>HyperCard is a software erector set. It lets people put things together without having to know how to solder.</p></blockquote>
<p>There’s a ton of information about HyperCard <a href="https://hypercard.org/">at hypercard.org</a>, including <a href="https://hcsimulator.com/">this HyperCard simulator</a> that runs in your browser.</p>
