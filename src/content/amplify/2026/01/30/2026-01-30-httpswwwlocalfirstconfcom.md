<h2>community tickets &amp; Sponsorship</h2>
<p><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdg-8uOPejsmEJswmwNaPzBkFZfZkyyfm9ooOxWPumPscxMSw/viewform">Submission closed</a></p>
<p>What</p>
<p>We have 15 <strong>free tickets</strong> available for anyone in our community who feels they need them. Three of those 15 tickets include a sponsorship, which covers travel and accommodation up to €1000.</p>
<p>Who?</p>
<p>We are trialling a self-nomination device that does not have any specific criteria. If you think you deserve a free ticket or sponsorship to attend, then please apply. We particularly encourage people from under-represented groups and those facing economic difficulties to apply.</p>
<p>How?</p>
<p>Nominations close on April 21st. We will review all applications as a team and decide on 12 people who will receive a free ticket. And a further 3 people who will receive a sponsorship. Our selection will be based on need and potential contribution to the community. The selected people will be announced by May 1st.</p>
<p>local-First Conf venue location</p>
<p>The event will take place at the Schankhalle Pfefferberg. The address is Schönhauser Allee 176, 10119 Berlin, Germany. This is the address of the main venue, we will be in the Theatersaal.</p>
