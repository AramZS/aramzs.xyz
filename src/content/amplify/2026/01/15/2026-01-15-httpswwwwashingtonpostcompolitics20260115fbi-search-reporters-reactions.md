<p>Many saw the FBI search of a Washington Post reporter’s home as a jarring new step aimed at limiting their ability to gather information.</p>
<p>After the <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/national-security/2026/01/14/washington-post-reporter-search/">FBI searched a Washington Post reporter’s home</a> Wednesday morning, reporters from multiple outlets said they moved swiftly to secure their phones and laptops, reassure confidential sources and consult newsroom leaders as they worried about the federal government’s seizure of devices containing sensitive information.</p>
