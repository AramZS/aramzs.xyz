<p><a href="https://see-saw.fun/tag/podcast/">Punk This Week</a><em> is see/saw’s free weekly podcast from co-hosts Evan Minsker and Nina Corcoran—a discussion of the latest and greatest in the world of punk rock. We talk about six new punk records we’re loving from the past week.</em></p>
<p>We try to open the podcast by saying “fuck ICE” in unison, which falls apart when Evan introduces the podcast as <em>Puck This Week</em>. Following a tangent about <em>Frasier, </em>“<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jl1vN-3jiT4&amp;ref=see-saw.fun">Grunge Frasier</a>,” the essential video of <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Eu-BqOsvj00&amp;ref=see-saw.fun">Kelsey Grammer falling off the stage</a>, and <em>Twilight Imperium</em>, we discuss new records by <a href="https://plantplantplant.bandcamp.com/album/evenimentul-major?ref=see-saw.fun">PLANT</a>, <a href="https://frequencescritiques.bandcamp.com/album/weak-fission?ref=see-saw.fun">Smetti Subito</a>, <a href="https://twothumbsdown.bandcamp.com/album/keena?ref=see-saw.fun">Two Thumbs Down</a>, <a href="https://generalspeech.bandcamp.com/album/running-b-w-vamos-a-ganar?ref=see-saw.fun">Disket</a>, <a href="https://hotface.bandcamp.com/album/automated-response?ref=see-saw.fun">Hot Face</a>, and <a href="https://recklessrelease.bandcamp.com/album/rr010-n-w-o-a-h-c-demo?ref=see-saw.fun">Tomcat</a>.</p>
<p>If you can, please <a href="https://linktr.ee/mplsmutualaid?ref=see-saw.fun">donate to some Minneapolis mutual aid funds amid the occupation</a>.</p>
