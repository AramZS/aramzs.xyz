<p>Tesla has recalled a total of 173 <a href="https://www.kbb.com/tesla/cybertruck/">Cybertruck</a> models from the 2024-2026 model year equipped with 18-inch steel wheels due to a potential wheel-stud failure.</p>
<p>Cracks in the brake rotor stud holes could cause the wheel studs to separate from the hub, making it harder to control the vehicle and potentially causing the wheel to fall off, increasing the likelihood of a crash.</p>
<p>Affected vehicles may exhibit vibration or noise prior to wheel stud separation. Tesla says it will completely replace the affected wheel hubs and rotors. The repairs will be conducted at no cost to owners, as mandated by law.</p>
