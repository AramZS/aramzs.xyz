<p><em>Michael </em>and <em>The Devil Wears Prada 2 </em>may be dominating theaters this month, but that doesn’t mean other movies aren’t hitting with audiences.</p>
<p>Take <em><a href="https://gizmodo.com/obsession-fantasic-fest-2025-review-curry-barker-horror-2000661992">Obsession</a>, </em>one of the weekend’s big new releases. Per <a href="https://variety.com/2026/film/box-office/box-office-michael-reclaims-first-place-obsession-starts-strong-1236751550/">Variety</a>, Curry Barker’s horror film opened to $23.1 million worldwide. The majority of it came from North America, making almost double industry projections and one of 2026’s biggest domestic start for a horror movie behind <em>Send Help </em>($19.1 million) and <em>Iron Lung </em>($17.8 million). Word of mouth was a big help in getting domestic audiences to see it, helped by a <a href="https://gizmodo.com/the-full-trailer-for-obsession-might-just-ruin-your-day-2000732385">strong marketing campaign</a> that includes a website for the <a href="https://www.onewishwillow.com/">One Wish Willow</a> that kicks the off the film’s plot. (No, you can’t buy it, and from the sound of things, this is a good thing for everyone.) For a movie that reportedly cost “<a href="https://www.newyorker.com/culture/persons-of-interest/the-twenty-six-year-old-behind-obsession-a-terrifying-tale-of-a-crush-gone-awry">$750,000 max</a>” to make, the movie’s more than made its money back and then some.</p>

<p>Even before it came out, <em>Obsession </em>seemed primed to be a standout among 2026’s early horror slate. It’s not just its positive reception now and at film festivals, it’s also that <a href="https://gizmodo.com/obsession-director-curry-barker-wants-to-push-the-boundaries-of-horror-itself-2000757150">Barker’s</a> got a built-in audience thanks to his past YouTube sketch comedy days and his directorial debut <em>Milk &amp; Serial. </em>And he’s not stopping here: Barker’s sticking with horror for the near future, starting with <em>Anything but Ghosts—</em>which he’ll star in alongside his sketch comedy partner Cooper Tomlinson and Aaron Paul—and continuing with A24’s movie revival for <em><a href="https://gizmodo.com/the-texas-chainsaw-massacre-reboot-snags-an-exciting-director-2000749339">Texas Chainsaw Massacre</a>.</em></p>
<p>Coming up this month, we’ve got <em>The Mandalorian and Grogu </em>on May 22, followed by <em>Backrooms </em>to things out on May 29. Even if <em>Obsession </em>gets buried under those two, it’s already won, and for many, will probably be deemed one of 2026’s best movies.</p>
