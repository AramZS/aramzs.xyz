<p>Beginning in May, we’re opening up the <a href="https://www.cortexrpg.com/">Cortex Prime RPG system</a> to both community and commercial projects, with direct digital distribution via DriveThruRPG.</p>
<p>Under the newly-released licensing agreement, only the largest and most successful commercial projects would have royalty obligations, giving independent creators more freedom to produce and share their work with the community.</p>
<p>The multi-year licensing deal will be administered by Cortex designer Cam Banks through a new partnership with his studio, Rusty Sellsword Design, giving independent creators a formal path to build and sell games “primed by Cortex.”</p>
<p>In addition to the new sublicense, Rusty Sellsword will be making additional Cortex Prime content available via DriveThruRPG in the coming months.</p>
<p>For licensing inquiries, please contact Rusty Sellsword at cam@rustysellsword.com.</p>
