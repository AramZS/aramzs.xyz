<p>For many years, it was the only Berlin restaurant with three Michelin stars. Despite Marco Müller's great creativity, the philosophy is down-to-earth: food, drink and hospitality.</p>
<figure><picture><figure><img src="https://falstaff.b-cdn.net/core/5744064/303529_lorenz-adlon-esszimmer_5744064.jpg?width=800"/></figure></picture></figure>
<p>Three menus determine the offer in the gastronomic mothership of cooking star Tim Raue: one Asian-inspired, one a Berlin mix with cosmopolitan local cuisine and one refined vegan.</p>
<figure><picture><figure><img src="https://falstaff.b-cdn.net/core/6455427/178013_horvth_6455427.jpg?width=800"/></figure></picture></figure>
<p>Top chef Sebastian Frank spoils his guests with vegetable cuisine of the highest standard, which pays homage to his Austrian homeland in particular. Sommelier Michi Stiel serves the matching drinks.</p>
<p>The region's finest brutally local produce: in Billy Wagner's restaurant, Micha Schäfer cooks with a keen sense for down-to-earth yet complex flavour combinations.</p>
