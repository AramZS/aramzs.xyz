<p>Five months before <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2025/10/13/us/alaska-flooding-rescues.html">catastrophic floods swept through</a> the Alaska Native village of Kipnuk on Sunday, tearing many houses off their foundations, the Trump administration canceled a $20 million grant intended to protect the community from such extreme flooding.</p>
<p>The grant from the Environmental Protection Agency was designed to help stabilize the riverbank on which Kipnuk is built, protecting it from the twin threats of erosion and flooding.</p>
