<p>The government shutdown barrels on. From my home in Texas, it’s easy to forget how many days it’s been going on.</p>
<p>I had a friend who was coming from DC to visit me over the weekend whose Congressional trip was canceled, but that’s been the extent of my interaction with it. Down here, it feels like the usual Washington dysfunction.</p>
