<p>ChatGPT-maker OpenAI’s new tool lets users make realistic AI videos of dead public figures. A rush of crude and racist memes has followed.</p>
<p>Ilyasah Shabazz didn’t want to look at the AI-generated videos of her father, Malcolm X. The seemingly realistic clips — made by OpenAI’s new video-maker <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2025/10/02/sora-openai-video-face-fake/">Sora 2</a> — show the legendary civil rights activist making crude jokes, wrestling with the Rev. Martin Luther King Jr. and talking about defecating on himself.</p>
