<p>Three years ago, before I learned that I had metastatic cancer, doctors repeatedly told me I was “too young” to be seriously ill.</p>
<p>I was 26.</p>
<p>My symptoms were brushed off as stress, bad luck or something I’d eventually get over. After more than five months of dead ends and dismissals, I even started questioning my sanity.</p>
