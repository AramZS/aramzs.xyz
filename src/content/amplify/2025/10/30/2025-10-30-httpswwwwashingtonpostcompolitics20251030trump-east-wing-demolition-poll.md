<p>Fifty-six percent say they oppose President Donald Trump’s decision to tear down part of the White House to build a 90,000-square-foot ballroom building.</p>
<p>Americans oppose President Donald Trump’s demolition of the White House’s East Wing to make way for a 90,000-square-foot ballroom building by a 2-to-1 margin, according to a <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/tablet/2025/10/30/oct-24-28-2025-washington-post-abc-news-ipsos-poll/">Washington Post-ABC News-Ipsos poll</a> released Thursday.</p>
