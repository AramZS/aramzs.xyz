<p>Historians and experts say the Trump administration’s efforts to deploy National Guard troops across the country could affect troop readiness, training and morale.</p>
<p>President Donald Trump is taking National Guard deployments into unprecedented territory, seeking to send troops into multiple U.S. cities despite strong opposition from local officials and questions about the purpose and impact of the mission.</p>
