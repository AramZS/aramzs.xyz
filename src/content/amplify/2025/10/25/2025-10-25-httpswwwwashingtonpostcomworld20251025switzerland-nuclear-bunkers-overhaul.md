<p>Citing “the changing global security situation,” the famously neutral country is embarking on a major revamp of personal shelters and civil protection sites.</p>
<p>Amid the charming cobbled streets and medieval buildings in Zurich’s old town, there is one site that never ceases to amaze guests of tour guide Samantha Aeschbach.</p>
<p>Urania, a cavernous underground space spanning seven stories, is a parking garage. But it’s also a modern military fortress hiding in plain sight, the owner of the Zurich Insider tour company tells her guests.</p>
