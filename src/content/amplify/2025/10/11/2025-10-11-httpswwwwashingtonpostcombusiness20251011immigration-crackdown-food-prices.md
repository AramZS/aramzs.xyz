<p>In an unusual acknowledgement, the Labor Department said that tougher immigration enforcement is hurting farmers and the food supply.</p>
<p>The Trump administration said that its immigration crackdown is hurting farmers and risking higher food prices for Americans by cutting off agriculture’s labor supply.</p>
<p>The Labor Department warned in an obscure <a href="https://www.federalregister.gov/d/2025-19365/p-240">document filed</a> with the Federal Register last week that “the near total cessation of the inflow of illegal aliens” is threatening “the stability of domestic food production and prices for U.S. consumers.”</p>
