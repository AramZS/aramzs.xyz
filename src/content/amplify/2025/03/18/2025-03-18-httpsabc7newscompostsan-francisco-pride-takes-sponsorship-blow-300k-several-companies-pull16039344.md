SAN FRANCISCO (KGO) -- San Francisco Pride is dealing with the loss of several corporate sponsors worth up to $300,000, according to Director Suzanne Ford. The companies include La Crema, Comcast, Anheuser Busch and Diageo. A fifth company hasn't been publicly revealed.
