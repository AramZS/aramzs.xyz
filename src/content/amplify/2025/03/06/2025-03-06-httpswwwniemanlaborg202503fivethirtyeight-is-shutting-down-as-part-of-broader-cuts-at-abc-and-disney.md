title: FiveThirtyEight is shutting down as part of broader cuts at ABC and Disney

Disney is cutting 200 positions across ABC News and Disney Entertainment, including shutting down FiveThirtyEight, The Wall Street Journal reported Tuesday night. The ABC news magazine shows “20/20” and “Nightline” are consolidating into one unit, resulting in job cuts, the people said.
