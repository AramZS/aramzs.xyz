<p>You should probably leave Substack. Here's why and how.</p>
<p>Unfortunately, Substack willingly platforms, and allows bad actors to monetize, hate speech and misinformation.</p>
<h2>Says Who?</h2>
<p>Here are some well-reasoned pieces on the subject for you to educate yourself and decide.</p>
<ul> <li><a href="https://digiday.com/media/former-substack-creators-say-theyre-earning-more-on-new-platforms-that-offer-larger-shares-of-subscription-revenue/">“Former Substack creators say they’re earning more on new platforms that offer larger shares of subscription revenue” - Digiday</a></li> <li><a href="https://nathantankus.substack.com/p/i-am-leaving-substack">“I Am Leaving Substack” - Notes on the Crises</a> describes additional issues around Substack users directly targeting and doxing people in marginalized groups.</li> </ul>
<h2>Where Can I Go, and How Can I Migrate?</h2>
<p>Below, in alphabetical order, are some guides to moving to competition that are better-suited for those who don’t want to platform hate speech and misinformation.</p>
<h3>General Advice</h3>
<ul> <li><a href="https://www.latterly.org/substack-competitors/">“Top Substack Competitors and Alternatives in 2025” - Latterly.org</a> - a great overview on how to think about which choice to make and good coverage of several alternatives.</li> </ul>
<p>Beehiiv is a commercial alternative to Substack, with a free tier.</p>
<p>Buttondown is a commercial alternative to Substack that has a free pricing tier. Many leaving Substack have chosen to migrate there.</p>
<p>Ghost is a non-profit, self-hosted publishing platform. It also has <a href="https://ghost.org/pricing/">hosted options</a> available for those who don’t want to self-host.</p>
<ul> <li><a href="https://www.readonlymemo.com/substack-to-ghost-migration-guide-in-2024-setting-up-mailgun-and-cloudflare/?ref=astrolabe.aidanmoher.com">“Substack to Ghost migration guide: How I imported my newsletter and set up Mailgun and Cloudflare” -Read Only Memo</a></li> <li><a href="https://bsky.app/profile/theradr.bsky.social/post/3knoaw5z4ek2v">A Bluesky thread</a> from a user who moved from Substack to Ghost.</li> </ul>
