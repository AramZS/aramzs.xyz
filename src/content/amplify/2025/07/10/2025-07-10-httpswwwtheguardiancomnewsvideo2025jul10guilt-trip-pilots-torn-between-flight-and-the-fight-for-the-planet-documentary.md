<h2 data-gu-name="standfirst"><p>Commercial pilots George Hibberd and Todd Smith grapple with the reality of their dream jobs, torn between childhood ambitions of flying and the impact of their industry on the world beneath them. As part of <a data-link-name="in standfirst link" href="https://safe-landing.org/">Safe Landing</a>, a community of aviation workers who want the industry to do better for the climate, they begin to transform their eco-anxiety and guilt into action.
