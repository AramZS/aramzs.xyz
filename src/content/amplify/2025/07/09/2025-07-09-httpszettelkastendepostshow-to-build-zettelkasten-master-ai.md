<blockquote> <p>“Now, here, you see, it takes all the running you can do, to keep in the same place. If you want to get somewhere else, you must run at least twice as fast as that!” - The Red Queen</p> </blockquote>
<p>Those who don’t learn to use AI, won’t be able to keep pace with those who do. AI writing is rattling the writer’s scene. It might be one of the strongest disruptors for the reason that it will be able to automate almost everything that isn’t truly original.</p>
<p>I strongly hold the belief that the same is true for the Zettelkasten Method: Those who don’t build an integrated thinking environment, won’t be able to keep pace with those who do.</p>
<p>I stumbled over a statement that perfectly express how AI and the Zettelkasten Method work together. The first one is:</p>
<blockquote> <p>You can’t automate what you can’t articulate.</p> </blockquote>
