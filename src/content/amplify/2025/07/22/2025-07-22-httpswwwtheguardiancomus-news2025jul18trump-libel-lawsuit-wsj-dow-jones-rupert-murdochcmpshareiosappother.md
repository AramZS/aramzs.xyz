<h2 data-gu-name="standfirst">Lawsuit filed in Miami federal court comes after president threatened to sue over Jeffrey Epstein letter report</h2>
<p>Donald Trump filed a lawsuit on Friday against Dow Jones, News Corp, Rupert Murdoch and two <a data-component="auto-linked-tag" data-link-name="in body link" href="https://www.theguardian.com/media/wallstreetjournal">Wall Street Journal</a> reporters, raising claims under federal libel law, court records show.</p>
<p>A copy of the complaint was not immediately available. The case was filed in Miami federal court.</p>
