<p>Owens disregarded three retraction requests after making “defamatory” allegations, according to the lawsuit.</p>
<p>French President Emmanuel Macron and his wife on Wednesday sued Candace Owens for defamation over the far-right commentator’s repeated allegations that first lady Brigitte Macron was born a man.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://clarelocke.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Complaint89.pdf">lawsuit</a>, filed in Delaware, alleges that Owens ignored the Macrons’ attempts to set the record straight in favor of mocking them and retaliated when they requested a retraction.</p>
