Pope Francis snubbed Vice President JD Vance when he visited the Vatican on Saturday, instead having his No. 2 give the vice president a lecture on compassion, according to a Vatican statement. Pope Francis was absent from Vance’s conversation with Cardinal Pietro Parolin.
