<p><a data-component="link" href="https://www.mtu.edu/computing/ai/">Artificial intelligence</a>, touted for its potential to transform medicine, led to some doctors losing skills after just a few months in a new study.</p>
<p>AI helped health professionals to better detect pre-cancerous growths in the colon, but when the assistance was removed, their ability to find tumors dropped by about 20% compared with rates before the tool was ever introduced, according to findings published Wednesday.</p>
