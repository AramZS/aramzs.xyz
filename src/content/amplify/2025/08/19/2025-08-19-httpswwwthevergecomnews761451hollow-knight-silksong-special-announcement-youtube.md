<p>Prepare your best clown makeup: Team Cherry, the developers of <em>Hollow Knight: Silksong</em>, will be making a “special announcement” about the game on Thursday at 10:30AM ET. The announcement will be shown on Team Cherry’s YouTube channel, and the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6XGeJwsUP9c">premiere link is already live</a>.</p>
<p>It’s been a very long wait for solid <em>Silksong</em> news, so much so that the waiting <a href="https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/hollow-knight-silksong-fans-in-clown-makeup">spawned a clown meme</a>. A <a href="https://www.theverge.com/2022/6/12/22175400/hollow-knight-silksong-sequel-xbox-game-pass-trailer">release window announcement</a> and <a href="https://www.theverge.com/2023/5/9/23717855/hollow-knight-silksong-delayed">subsequent delay</a> didn’t help things. But Nintendo shared proof-of-life for <em>Silksong</em> earlier this year when it snuck <a href="https://www.theverge.com/news/610766/hollow-knight-silksong-release-date-trailer">a vague “2025” release date</a> into its big Switch 2 Direct presentation.</p>
<p>And as of late, there have been a lot of hints that some big <em>Silksong</em> news is imminent: the game will be shown at both <a href="https://www.theverge.com/news/756538/silksong-will-also-at-nintendos-gamescom-booth">Nintendo</a> and <a href="https://www.theverge.com/news/714490/xbox-gamescom-silksong-playable-demo-xbox-ally-handheld">Xbox’s</a> Gamescom booths this week, and on Monday, Gamescom Opening Night Live host Geoff Keighley tweeted a picture of himself <a href="https://x.com/geoffkeighley/status/1957492830374650018">wearing a clown nose</a>. However, the game’s release date won’t be announced during <a href="https://www.theverge.com/news/761027/gamescom-opening-night-live-2025-geoff-keighley-how-to-watch">Tuesday’s Opening Night Live show</a>, <a href="https://bsky.app/profile/jasonschreier.bsky.social/post/3lwr4xi2x5c2g">according to <em>Bloomberg’s</em> Jason Schreier</a>.</p>
<p>I’m unrealistically hoping that <em>Silksong</em> releases on Thursday. I’m on vacation next week, which would give me a perfect excuse to dive into the game. But if the release date is a little bit after, no biggie — maybe I’ll get lost in the original <em>Hollow Knight</em> one more time.</p>
