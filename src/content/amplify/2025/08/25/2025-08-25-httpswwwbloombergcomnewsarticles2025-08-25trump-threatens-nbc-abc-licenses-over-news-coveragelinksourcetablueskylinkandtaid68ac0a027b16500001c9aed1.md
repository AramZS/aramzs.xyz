<p>US President Donald Trump berated NBC and ABC as “two of the worst and most biased networks in history,” adding he’d support the FCC in revoking licenses to their television stations.</p>
<p>The networks “give me 97% BAD STORIES,” Trump said in <a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/news/terminal/T1J1K2BSWSG0">a Truth Social post</a> on Sunday, “despite a very high popularity and, according to many, among the greatest 8 months in Presidential History.”</p>
