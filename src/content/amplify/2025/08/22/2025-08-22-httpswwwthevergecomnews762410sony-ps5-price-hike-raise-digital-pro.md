<p>Sony is raising the price of all PS5 models by $50 in the US due to a “challenging economic environment,” according to <a href="https://blog.playstation.com/2025/08/20/playstation-5-price-changes-in-the-u-s/">a blog post</a>. The changes will go into effect on Thursday.</p>
<p>The new prices are as follows:</p>
<ul><li>PlayStation 5 – $549.99</li><li>PlayStation 5 Digital Edition – $499.99</li><li>PlayStation 5 Pro – $749.99</li></ul>
