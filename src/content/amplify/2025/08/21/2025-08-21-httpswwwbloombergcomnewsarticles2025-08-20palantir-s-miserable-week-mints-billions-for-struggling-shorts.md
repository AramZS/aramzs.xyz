<p><a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/PLTR:US">Palantir Technologies Inc.</a>’s six-session stock-market losing streak has wiped out $73 billion in market value, handing a rare win to short sellers who are getting pounded after betting against this year’s Wall Street juggernaut.</p>
<p>Since hitting a record on Aug. 12, shares of the data analysis and software firm are down more than 17%. It’s their longest losing streak since April 2024, putting the stock on track for its worst week since the tariff tantrum in early April of this year.</p>
