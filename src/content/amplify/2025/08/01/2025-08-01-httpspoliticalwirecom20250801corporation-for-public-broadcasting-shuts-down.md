<p><a data-wpel-link="internal" href="https://politicalwire.com/about-taegan-goddard">Taegan Goddard</a> is the founder of <a data-wpel-link="internal" href="https://politicalwire.com">Political Wire</a>, one of the earliest and most influential political web sites. He also runs <a data-wpel-link="external" href="http://www.politicaljobhunt.com">Political Job Hunt</a>, <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://electoralvotemap.com">Electoral Vote Map</a> and the <a data-wpel-link="external" href="http://politicaldictionary.com">Political Dictionary</a>.</p>
<p>Goddard spent more than a decade as managing director and chief operating officer of a prominent investment firm in New York City. Previously, he was a policy adviser to a U.S. Senator and Governor.</p>
<p>Goddard is also co-author of <a data-wpel-link="external" href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/0684852039/ref=nosim?tag=youwonnowwhat&amp;link_code=as3&amp;creativeASIN=0684852039&amp;creative=373489&amp;camp=211189"><em>You Won - Now What?</em></a> (Scribner, 1998), a political management book hailed by prominent journalists and politicians from both parties. In addition, Goddard's essays on politics and public policy have appeared in dozens of newspapers across the country.</p>
<p>Goddard earned degrees from Vassar College and Harvard University. He lives in New York with his wife and three sons.</p>
<p>Goddard is the owner of <em>Goddard Media LLC</em>.</p>
