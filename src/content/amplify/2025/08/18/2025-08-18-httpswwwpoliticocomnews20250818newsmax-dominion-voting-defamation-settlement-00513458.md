<p>As part of the agreement, the first payment of $27 million was paid Aug. 15. Two more $20 million payments must be paid in 2026 and 2027, <a data-tracking="mpos=center&amp;mid=ar_body&amp;lindex=5&amp;lcol=" href="https://www.sec.gov/ix?doc=/Archives/edgar/data/0002026478/000121390025077611/ea025361001-8k_newsmax.htm#fact-identifier-18">according to a filing</a> from the Securities and Exchange Commission.</p>
<p>In a statement, Newsmax defended its coverage as “fair, balanced, and conducted within professional standards of journalism.”</p>
<p>“The actions taken against Newsmax, and earlier against Fox News, represent a direct attack on free speech and a free press,” Newsmax CEO Christopher Ruddy said in a statement.</p>
<p>Earlier this year, Fox News <a data-tracking="mpos=center&amp;mid=ar_body&amp;lindex=6&amp;lcol=" href="https://www.politico.com/news/2023/04/18/fox-news-reaches-settlement-with-dominion-in-defamation-lawsuit-00092621">reached a $787 million settlement</a> with Dominion over similar claims. Newsmax <a data-tracking="mpos=center&amp;mid=ar_body&amp;lindex=7&amp;lcol=" href="https://www.politico.com/news/2024/09/26/smartmatic-newsmax-settlement-2020-election-defamation-00181264">previously settled</a> with Smartmatic, another voting machine company, over defamation claims in 2024.</p>
