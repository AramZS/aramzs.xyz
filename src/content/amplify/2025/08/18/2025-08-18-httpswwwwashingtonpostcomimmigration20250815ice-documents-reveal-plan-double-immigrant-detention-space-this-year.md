<p>Billions will be spent to reach more than 107,000 beds, including in mega-facilities, tents and at least two new family detention centers, according to an internal agency roadmap.</p>
<p>When President Donald Trump took office this year, the United States already commanded the <a href="https://www.globaldetentionproject.org/countries/americas/united-states">largest</a> immigrant detention system in the world with a capacity of close to 50,000 migrants. Right away, his administration set a goal of doubling it.</p>
