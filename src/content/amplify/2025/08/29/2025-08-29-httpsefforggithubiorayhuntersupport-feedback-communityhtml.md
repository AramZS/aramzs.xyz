<ul> <li>If you've received a Rayhunter warning and would like to help us with our research, please send your Rayhunter data captures (QMDL and PCAP logs) to us at our <a href="https://signal.org/">Signal</a> username <a href="https://signal.me/#eu/HZbPPED5LyMkbTxJsG2PtWc2TXxPUR1OxBMcJGLOPeeCDGPuaTpOi5cfGRY6RrGf"><strong>ElectronicFrontierFoundation.90</strong></a> with the following information: capture date, capture location, device, device model, and Rayhunter version. If you're unfamiliar with Signal, feel free to check out our <a href="https://ssd.eff.org/module/how-to-use-signal">Security Self Defense guide on it</a>.</li> <li>If you're having issues installing or using Rayhunter, please <a href="https://github.com/EFForg/rayhunter/issues">open an issue</a> on our Github repo.</li> <li>If you'd like to propose a feature, heuristic, or device for Rayhunter, <a href="https://github.com/EFForg/rayhunter/discussions">start a discussion</a> in our Github repo</li> <li>For anything else, join us in the <code>#rayhunter</code> or <code>#rayhunter-developers</code> channel of <a href="https://opensource.eff.org/signup_user_complete/?id=r1b6cnta9bysxk6im3kuabiu1y&amp;md=link&amp;sbr=su">EFF's Mattermost</a> instance to chat!</li> </ul>
