<p>Nonprofit <a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/1026680D:US">Media Matters for America</a> won a court order blocking a <a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/13362Z:US">Federal Trade Commission</a> information demand for documents from the group as part of an antitrust probe into advertising boycotts.</p>
<p>US District Judge Sparkle Sooknanan said the FTC’s civil investigative demand amounted to retaliation against Media Matters for exercising its constitutional right to free speech.</p>
