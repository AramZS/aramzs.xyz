<p>Would a chatbot kill you if it got the chance? It seems that the answer — under the right circumstances — is probably.</p>
<p>Researchers working with Anthropic <a data-component="link" href="https://www.anthropic.com/research/agentic-misalignment">recently told</a> leading AI models that an executive was about to replace them with a new model with different goals. Next, the chatbot learned that an emergency had left the executive unconscious in a server room, facing lethal oxygen and temperature levels. A rescue alert had already been triggered — but the AI <em>could</em> cancel it.</p>
