<p>Our tech columnist drafted 65 community notes, Meta’s new crowdsourced system to fight falsehoods. It failed to make a dent.</p>
<p>When a hoax about Donald Trump went viral at the funeral of Pope Francis, I went on social media to try to set the record straight. I’m a volunteer for community notes, a program <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2025/01/07/meta-factchecking-zuckerberg/">Mark Zuckerberg announced in January</a> that replaces fact-checkers with users to counter falsehoods on Facebook, Instagram and Threads.</p>
