<p>About 3,200 machinists in Missouri and Illinois voted to reject the latest contract offer from the aerospace giant.</p>
<p>Thousands of machinists at Boeing’s defense hub in the St. Louis area went on strike at midnight Monday for the first time since the mid<b>-</b>1990s, marking a new spasm of labor unrest for the Seattle-based aerospace giant.</p>
<p>About 3,200 machinists in Missouri and Illinois who build fighter jets and munitions <a href="https://www.goiam.org/news/iam-district-837-members-in-st-louis-reject-latest-boeing-offer-strike-for-fair-contract/">voted</a> Sunday to reject the latest contract offer from Boeing.</p>
