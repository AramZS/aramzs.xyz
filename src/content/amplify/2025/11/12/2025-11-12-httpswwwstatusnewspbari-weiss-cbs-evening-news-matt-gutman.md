<p>In her first month as editor in chief of <strong>CBS News</strong>, <strong>Bari Weiss</strong> has been actively scouting for talent to help redefine the network’s identity. Weiss has personally reached out, by phone and text, to a number of high-profile journalists across major outlets, including <strong>Fox News </strong>and <strong>CNN</strong>, sounding them out about potential roles at the <strong>Tiffany Network </strong>and gauging their interest in joining the newsroom she is building.</p>

With Dickerson <a href="https://www.status.news/p/john-dickerson-cbs-evening-news-resigns">set to depart </a>by year’s end, the decision over who will helm the network’s flagship newscast looms large.</p>
