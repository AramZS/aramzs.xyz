<p>WASHINGTON (AP) — President Donald Trump has pardoned his former personal lawyer Rudy Giuliani, his former chief of staff <a data-gtm-enhancement-style="LinkEnhancementA" href="https://apnews.com/hub/mark-meadows">Mark Meadows</a> and others accused of backing the Republican’s efforts to overturn the 2020 election, a Justice Department official says.</p>
<p>Ed Martin, the government’s pardon attorney, posted on social media a signed proclamation of the “full, complete, and unconditional” pardon, which also names conservative attorneys <a data-gtm-enhancement-style="LinkEnhancementA" href="https://apnews.com/article/sidney-powell-plea-deal-georgia-election-indictment-ec7dc601ad78d756643aa2544028e9f5">Sidney Powell</a> and <a data-gtm-enhancement-style="LinkEnhancementA" href="https://apnews.com/article/arizona-fake-electors-charges-john-eastman-2020-1d6df9ac00b810a0d54d6fc4ae97ec57">John Eastman</a>. The proclamation, posted online late Sunday, explicitly says the pardon does not apply to Trump.</p>
<p>Presidential pardons apply only to federal crimes, and none of the Trump allies was charged in a federal case. But the move underscores Trump’s efforts to continue to rewrite the history of the 2020 election he lost to Democrat Joe Biden.</p>
<p>The White House didn’t immediately respond to an email seeking comment Monday.</p>
<p>Also pardoned were Republicans who acted as <a data-gtm-enhancement-style="LinkEnhancementA" href="https://apnews.com/article/trump-2020-election-fake-electors-michigan-e96dd694562a4b5903201ff538eca989">fake electors</a> for Trump in 2020 and were charged in state cases of submitting false certificates that confirmed they were legitimate electors despite Biden’s victory in those states.</p>
<p>The proclamation described efforts to prosecute those who were involved in the 2020 election schemes “as a grave national injustice perpetrated on the American people” and said the pardons were designed to continue “the process of national reconciliation.”</p>
