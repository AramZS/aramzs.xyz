<p>Trump also warned of aid cuts over attacks on Christians, though an adviser to Nigeria’s president dismissed the remarks as a negotiating tactic.</p>
<p>DAKAR, Senegal — Nigerians described a mixture of confusion and fear on Sunday as people across the country tried to decipher <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/2025/11/01/trump-nigeria-christians/">President Donald Trump’s threat</a> to stop all aid to Africa’s most populous country and go in “guns-a-blazing” after accusing Nigeria’s government of allowing “the killing of Christians.”</p>
