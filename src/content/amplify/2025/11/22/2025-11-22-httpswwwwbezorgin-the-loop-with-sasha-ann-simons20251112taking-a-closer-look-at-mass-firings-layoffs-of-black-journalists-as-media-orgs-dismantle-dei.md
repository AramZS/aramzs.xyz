<p>Dozens of Black journalists have been <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://afrotech.com/black-cbs-employee-alleges-people-of-color-were-laid-off"><u>dismissed</u></a> <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://deadline.com/2025/11/conde-nast-fires-staffers-teen-vogue-1236609695/"><u>in</u></a> <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://www.npr.org/2025/02/25/g-s1-50551/joy-reid-msnbc-fired"><u>recent</u></a> <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://nabjonline.org/blog/nbcnewsmeeting/"><u>months</u></a> as <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://www.npr.org/2025/02/11/nx-s1-5293292/pbs-shutters-dei-office-diversity-equity-inclusion-trump-executive-order"><u>media</u></a> and news organizations <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/sep/14/rightwing-news-media-journalism"><u>seemingly roll back</u></a> their commitments to increased diversity in newsrooms. <i>In the Loop </i>speaks to a panel of local journalists about the contributions of Black-led journalism, and how systemic biases <a data-cms-ai="0" href="https://www.phillytrib.com/news/exodus-of-black-journalists-at-the-washington-post-brings-fear-of-silencing-voices-of-diversity/article_91df49ca-a45c-4233-8dbe-ea18171f1f16.html"><u>suppress journalists</u></a> and the communities they cover.</p>
