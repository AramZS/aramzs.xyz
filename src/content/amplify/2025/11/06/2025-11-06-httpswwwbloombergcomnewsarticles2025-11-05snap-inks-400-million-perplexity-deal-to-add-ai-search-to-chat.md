<p><a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/SNAP:US">Snap Inc.</a> announced a $400 million partnership with <a data-component="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/2235574D:US">Perplexity AI Inc.</a> to distribute the firm’s AI search engine to Snapchat users globally, according to a statement Wednesday. The shares surged 16% in extended trading after the news.</p>
<p>The deal gives Snap a new business line and puts the social messaging company more squarely into the mix around AI chatbots and assistants. Perplexity’s AI-powered search engine will take “default” placement inside of Snapchat beginning in 2026, according to Snap Chief Executive Officer Evan Spiegel, though the company’s existing My AI chatbot will also be available to users.</p>
