<h1>Intro</h1>
<p><strong>Digital Credentials Developer</strong> is a resource for developers looking to implement issuance and presentation of verifiable digital credentials using the Digital Credentials API.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.nist.gov/blogs/cybersecurity-insights/digital-identities-getting-know-verifiable-digital-credential-ecosystem">Verifiable digital credentials</a> are tamper-evident, cryptographically signed digital records that allow individuals to securely hold and share claims. These credentials offer a standardized and interoperable way to represent information, such as educational qualifications, professional certifications, or membership statuses.</p>
<p>Here, you will find resources for:</p>
<ul> <li>Issuing verifiable digital credentials via the Digital Credentials API</li> <li>Requesting presentation of verifiable digital credentials via the Digital Credentials API</li> <li>Platform and ecosystem support for APIs and protocols</li> </ul>
