<p>The opinion journalist took over as editor in chief in October, launching a new era for the network amid layoffs, confusion and shifting expectations.</p>
<p>Two journalists who are part of Bari Weiss’s expanding journalistic empire were on-site to cover now-Mayor-elect Zohran Mamdani’s election night watch party Tuesday in Brooklyn. Ed O’Keefe, senior White House reporter for CBS News, approached the event with the signature buttoned-up style of a longtime network political correspondent. Also present was Olivia Reingold, an election-night contributor to CBS who is a staff writer for the Free Press, the popular opinion website co-founded by Weiss, who was <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/2025/10/06/bari-weiss-cbs-paramount/">named CBS News editor in chief </a>last month.</p>
