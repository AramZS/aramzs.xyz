<p>The Democratic victory marks a concrete response to the president’s political agenda less than a year into his second term.</p>
<p>Democratic officials in Washington and Texas wanted Gavin Newsom’s help.</p>
<p>They reached out to the California governor’s team in early July to ask if he could use his growing social media megaphone to push back on President Donald Trump and Texas Republicans, who were barreling toward redrawing the congressional map to their advantage. Newsom was happy to oblige.</p>
