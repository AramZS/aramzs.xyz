<p>Japanese giant <a href="https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/">SoftBank</a> said Tuesday it has sold its entire stake in tech giant <a href="https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/NVDA/">Nvidia</a> for $5.83 billion.</p>
<p>The firm said in its earning statement that it sold 32.1 million shares of Nvidia in October. It also sold off part of its stake in T-Mobile for $9.17 billion.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/9984.T-JP/">SoftBank</a>'s investments in ChatGPT maker OpenAI and PayPay helped the Japanese giant <a href="https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/11/softbank-earnings-report-2q.html">post a $19 billion gain</a> on its Vision Fund in its fiscal second quarter.</p>
