<p>The Trump administration announced plans to roll back protections for plants and animals under the Endangered Species Act.</p>
<p>The Trump administration <a href="https://www.doi.gov/pressreleases/administration-revises-endangered-species-act-regulations-strengthen-certainty">announced</a> proposals Wednesday to roll back protections for plants and animals under the Endangered Species Act, a bedrock environmental law credited with rescuing the bald eagle, American alligator, peregrine falcon and numerous other species from extinction.</p>
