US stock index futures tumbled in early Asian trading hours Monday amid concerns that an artificial intelligence model from China’s DeepSeek may disrupt the tech market. S&P 500 futures fell as much as 1% as of 10:12 a.m. in Hong Kong, while contracts on the Nasdaq 100 dropped as much as 1.9%.
