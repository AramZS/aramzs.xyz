<p>December 31, 2025 at 5:00 a.m. ESTToday at 5:00 a.m. EST</p>
<p>President Donald Trump in mid-September quietly launched one of the biggest transformations to the White House in its 233-year history, concealing plans that were far more sweeping than what he had revealed publicly.</p>
<p>Its location was largely hidden, enclosed by the U.S. Treasury, East Wing and the main residence.</p>
