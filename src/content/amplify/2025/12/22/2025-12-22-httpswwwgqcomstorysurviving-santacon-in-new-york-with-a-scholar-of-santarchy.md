<p>Organizers describe SantaCon—the annual bar crawl in which thousands of revelers dressed as Santa and other <a href="https://www.gq.com/story/vintage-holiday-gift-guide">holiday</a> characters flood the streets of New York—as “a charitable, non-political, nonsensical Santa Claus convention” spreading absurdist joy. Most New Yorkers, on the other hand, consider SantaCon the worst day of the calendar year, a nightmarish bridge-and-tunnel invasion. John Oliver <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_TfCgeYHiBE">has called it</a> “a terrifying combination of binge-drinking, public urination, and trauma to small children that decades of therapy will never manage to reverse.” Oh, and don’t forget the vomit and <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1i9-jEm2hIw">street fights</a>.</p>
