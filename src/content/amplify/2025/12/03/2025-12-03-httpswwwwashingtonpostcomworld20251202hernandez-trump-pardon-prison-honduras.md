<p>Juan Orlando Hernández was serving a 45-year sentence over drug trafficking. Following a pardon from President Donald Trump, he has been released.</p>
<p>Former Honduran president Juan Orlando Hernández, who was convicted in a U.S. court of running a “narco-state” that helped send South American cocaine to the United States, has been released from federal prison days after President Donald Trump said he would <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/2025/11/28/trump-pardon-honduras-hernandez/">issue a pardon</a>.</p>
