<p>The university claims it preserved academic autonomy. It didn’t.</p>
<p>Let’s suppose a robber puts a gun to your head and demands your wallet. You don’t want to give it to him, but you also don’t have a choice. So you cough up the wallet.</p>
<p>I get that. But don’t tell me that everything is fine now because you’re free to go about your life. We both know that’s not true. And lying about it won’t make it any better.</p>
