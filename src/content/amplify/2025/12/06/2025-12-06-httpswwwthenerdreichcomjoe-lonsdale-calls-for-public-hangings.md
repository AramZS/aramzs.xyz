(photo: TechCrunch/Wikimedia Commons)</figcaption></figure>
<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Lonsdale?ref=thenerdreich.com">Joe Lonsdale</a>, a billionaire member of the “<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PayPal_Mafia?ref=thenerdreich.com">PayPal Mafia</a>” who is also a co-founder of Palantir, wants a return to draconian punishment and <a href="https://x.com/gilduran76/status/1997378969004621902?s=20&amp;ref=thenerdreich.com">public executions</a> in the United States.</p>
<p>Wrote Lonsdale <a href="https://x.com/JTLonsdale/status/1996947600533066185?s=20&amp;ref=thenerdreich.com">on X</a> yesterday:</p>
<blockquote>If I'm in charge later, we won't just have a three strikes law. We will quickly try and hang men after three violent crimes. <br/><br/>And yes, we will do it in public to deter others. <br/><br/>Our society needs balance. It’s time to bring back masculine leadership to protect our most vulnerable.</blockquote>
<figure><img alt="" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px" src="https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-10.33.42---AM.png" srcset="https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/size/w600/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-10.33.42---AM.png 600w, https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/size/w1000/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-10.33.42---AM.png 1000w, https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-10.33.42---AM.png 1194w"/></figure>
<p>The venture capitalist was responding to a writer who had criticized Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth’s enthusiastic tweets about murdering people on boats in the Caribbean.</p>
<p>On December 4, <a href="https://x.com/AndrewKolvet/status/1996706611029532744?s=20&amp;ref=thenerdreich.com">Andrew Kolvet</a>—a spokesman for Charlie Kirk’s Turning Point USA—praised Hegseth for his bombing campaign against fishing boats, which he accuses of drug smuggling. Critics say <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/dec/04/venezuela-boat-strikes-legality-hegseth?ref=thenerdreich.com">the bombings</a>, which have killed scores of people without due process, may constitute <a href="https://thanedar.house.gov/media/press-releases/congressman-shri-thanedar-introduces-articles-of-impeachment-against-secretary-of-defense-pete-hegseth?ref=thenerdreich.com">murder</a> or war crimes.</p>
<p>“Every new attack aimed at Pete Hegseth makes me want another narco drug boat blown up and sent to the bottom of the ocean,” wrote Kolvet.</p>
<p>“Your wish is our command, Andrew. Just sunk another narco boat,” <a href="https://x.com/PeteHegseth/status/1996757617427824861?s=20&amp;ref=thenerdreich.com">replied Hegseth</a>.</p>
<figure><img alt="" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px" src="https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-12.34.17---PM-1.png" srcset="https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/size/w600/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-12.34.17---PM-1.png 600w, https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/size/w1000/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-12.34.17---PM-1.png 1000w, https://www.thenerdreich.com/content/images/2025/12/Screenshot-2025-12-06-at-12.34.17---PM-1.png 1201w"/></figure>
<p>When Australian writer Claire Lehmann criticized Hegseth’s post as “grotesque,” Lonsdale responded, declaring that sinking the boats “helps publicly deter others” and “killing bad guys” is Hegseth’s job.</p>
<p>“Masculine truth: bold, virtuous men deter evil,” wrote Lonsdale.</p>
<p>In his subsequent post, he unveiled his fantasy of a future in which he, put in charge, erodes due process, expands the use of the death penalty, and revives public hangings.</p>
<p>This public fetishization of state violence—framed explicitly as “masculine truth” and virtuous protection—suggests tech elites are entering a more dangerous and desperate phase of radicalization.</p>
<p>For months, Peter Thiel guru <a href="https://www.thenerdreich.com/curtis-yarvin-fears-his-authoritarian-fantasy-is-flopping/">Curtis Yarvin</a> has been squawking about the need for more severe measures to cement Trump's authoritarian rule. Peter Thiel is ranting about <a href="https://www.thenerdreich.com/peter-thiel-the-antichrist-silicon-valley-apocalypse-hype/">the Antichrist</a> in a global tour. And now Lonsdale—a Thiel protégé—is fantasizing about a future in which he will have the power to unleash state violence at mass scale.</p>
<p>The meme is spreading. Where is it heading?</p>
