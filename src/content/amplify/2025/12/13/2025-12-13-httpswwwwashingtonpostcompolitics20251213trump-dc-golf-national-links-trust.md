<p>The administration puts the National Links Trust on the clock in a bid to take control of the city’s three public golf courses.</p>
<p>The Trump administration has issued a notice of default to the group that manages Washington’s three municipal golf courses, escalating a behind-the-scenes struggle over who will control some of the District’s most visible public land and effectively positioning the president to operate its public golf properties.</p>
