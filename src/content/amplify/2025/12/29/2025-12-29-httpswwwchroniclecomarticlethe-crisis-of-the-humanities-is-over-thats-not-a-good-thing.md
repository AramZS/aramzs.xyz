<p>All of higher ed now suffers the attacks of politics and technology.</p>
<p>The good news: There is no longer a crisis in the humanities. Our field’s long-running narrative of continuous crisis is over. The bad news: The crisis of the humanities has been revealed by the events of the last year to be a crisis of civil society writ large.</p>
