<h2>People on the supersonic Concorde in 1973 saw an eclipse for 74 minutes</h2>
<figure><img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i0.wp.com/newsletter.mathewingram.com/content/images/2025/12/17670232697471782351180778191478.jpg?w=525&amp;ssl=1"/></figure>
<p>On 30 June 1973, the supersonic jet Concorde 001 intercepted the path of a total solar eclipse and followed the path of totality as it crossed Africa.
