<h2>What This Means</h2>
<p>During the technical preview period:</p>
<ul> <li><strong>Core functionality is stable</strong> - You can create profiles, support creators, and manage your account</li> <li><strong>Data is persistent</strong> - Your supporter records and profile data are stored permanently in the ATProtocol network</li> <li><strong>Some features may change</strong> - We may adjust functionality based on user feedback</li> <li><strong>Bugs may exist</strong> - While we've tested extensively, edge cases may still cause unexpected behavior</li> </ul>
