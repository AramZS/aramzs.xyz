<p>The chatbots made by users of Character.AI used recognizable synthetic voices for the stars.</p>
<p>Character.AI became one of the world’s <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2024/12/06/ai-companion-chai-research-character-ai/">most popular artificial intelligence apps</a> by letting tens of millions of users, many in their teens, text and talk to chatbot versions of celebrities and fictional characters. But those friendly chatbots can easily veer into topics unsafe for minors.</p>
