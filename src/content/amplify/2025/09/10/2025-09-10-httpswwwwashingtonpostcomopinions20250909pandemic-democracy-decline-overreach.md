<p>The pandemic has accelerated a global decline in democracy that countries must now address.</p>
<p><i>Joseph Lemoine is senior director and Will Mortenson is a program assistant at the Atlantic Council’s Freedom and Prosperity Center.</i></p>
<p>When covid-19 first swept the globe, the immediate concern was public health. International supply chains were thrown into chaos, and political leaders were faced with the prospect of restricting social behavior to safeguard public health.</p>
