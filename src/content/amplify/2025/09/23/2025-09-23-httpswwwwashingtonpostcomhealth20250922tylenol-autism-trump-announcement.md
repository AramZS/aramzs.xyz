<p>U.S. officials will issue a warning label for pregnant individuals — a move that contradicts the guidance from the major medical societies.</p>
<p>The Trump administration on Monday ventured into uncertain scientific territory, calling to limit the use of one medication under investigation for a possible connection to autism while pushing to expand access to another being studied as a potential therapy — despite the lack of definitive evidence for either.</p>
