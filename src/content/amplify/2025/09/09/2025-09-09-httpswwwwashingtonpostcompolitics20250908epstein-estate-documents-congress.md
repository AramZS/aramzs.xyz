<p>The House Oversight Committee received documents it subpoenaed from financier and convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein’s estate on Monday. The committee is in the midst of an inquiry into the federal government’s Epstein sex-trafficking investigation, that aims to dispel lingering questions about the scope of Epstein’s sex abuse and his death.</p>
