<p>Bizarre videos, uncanny photos, and Luigi Mangoine's likeness on Shein...? AI slop is taking over the web. It's putting money in people's pockets, and driving them offline, too.</p>
<p>This is <em>AI + U</em>. Each Monday this month, Brittany is exploring how you are already seeing the impacts of AI. Artificial Intelligence has become a constant in ways we can and can't see...and for the next few weeks we're zeroing in on how AI affects our daily lives.</p>
<p>Brittany chats with Washington Post tech reporter <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/people/drew-harwell/">Drew Harwell</a> and freelance writer <a href="https://emmamarris.com/">Emma Marris</a> about the limits of AI creativity and what this 'slop' is doing to us on and offline.</p>
