<p>Convenience stores in Asia give new meaning to the term: Customers can pay bills, ship packages, eat a nutritious meal and — in some locations — work out.</p>
<p>TAIPEI, Taiwan — Need to ship an international package, get clothes dry-cleaned or download a government tax certificate at 3 a.m.? Want to buy a train or movie ticket, then grab a tray of noodles you can heat and eat? How about a cold draft beer while you’re at it? In Taiwan, convenience stores have you covered.</p>
