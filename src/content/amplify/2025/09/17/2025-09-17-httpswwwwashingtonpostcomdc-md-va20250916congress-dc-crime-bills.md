<p>Republicans in Congress are pursuing a wave of new laws aimed at D.C.’s criminal justice system.</p>
<p>House lawmakers voted Tuesday to allow 14-year-olds to be tried as adults for serious crimes and to treat young people more harshly in the D.C. justice system — fulfilling a top request from the Trump administration despite universal opposition among top D.C. elected officials.</p>
