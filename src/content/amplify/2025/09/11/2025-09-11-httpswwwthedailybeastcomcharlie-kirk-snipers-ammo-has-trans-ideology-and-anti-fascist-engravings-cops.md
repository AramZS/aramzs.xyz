Journal</i></a>.</p><p>Citing “an internal law enforcement bulletin and a person familiar with the investigation,” the paper reported that the ammunition was found inside a .30 caliber hunting rifle that was ditched in a wooded area at Utah Valley University, where the conservative activist was shot dead as he spoke at event. </p><div><p>The <i>Journal</i>’s sources said the rifle was wrapped in
