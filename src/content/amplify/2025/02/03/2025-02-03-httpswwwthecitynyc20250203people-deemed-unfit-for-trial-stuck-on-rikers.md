title: Scores of People Deemed Unfit to Stand Trial Are Stuck on Rikers Island

At least 127 people found unfit to stand trial by psychiatrists are languishing on Rikers Island because there is no space at state-run mental health facilities, THE CITY has learned. Jail insiders say that number has gone up in recent years, after usually hovering around 100.
