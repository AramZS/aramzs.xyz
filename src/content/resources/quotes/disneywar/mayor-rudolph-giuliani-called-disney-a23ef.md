> Mayor Rudolph Giuliani called Disney and Times Square a “match made in heaven” and predicted that Disney’s involvement would jump-start the redevelopment of Times Square. In a few months, other companies and developers followed Disney’s lead, including Tishman Realty, Disney’s partners in the Dolphin and Swan Hotels; Condé Nast, publisher of Vogue and Vanity Fair; and the AMC Entertainment movie theater chain.
