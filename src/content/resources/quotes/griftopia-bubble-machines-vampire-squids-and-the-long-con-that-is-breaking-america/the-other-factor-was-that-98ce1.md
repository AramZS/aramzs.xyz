> The other factor was that there were no regulations that dictated that Bank B had to have any money at all before it offered to sell this CDS insurance. In other words, Bank A could take out insurance on its IBM holdings with Bank B and get an exemption from lending restrictions from regulators, even if Bank B never actually posted any money or proved that it could cover that bet.
