> It turns out the arsonists are foot soldiers of the Guardians of America, a secretive group of Nazi-like terrorists run by a racist and religious bigot who goes by the name Frank Hill. Hill and his gang of teenage thugs are out to stop the Metropolis Interfaith Council from building a community clubhouse and gymnasium “for the use of all boys and girls in the neighborhood, regardless of race, creed or color … where children of every race, ethnic background and spiritual belief can play and learn that all people are alike.”
