> CDC continues to say this is not a pandemic . . . My response is that a pandemic is not defined by what is happening in the US—it is defined by what is happening across the world (pan = all, demic = people, all people) . . . I know that this is not CDC’s intent but it is creating problems for bureaucrats who suffer from malignant obedience.”
