> If a virus mutated into something different, or worse, all the health officers could know at once, and adapt. The new web of information could create a thing that the country badly needed: a true network. Where once they had been a bunch of poorly connected dots on the map there would grow a tight web. A system. “It’s the future of disease control,” said Charity.
