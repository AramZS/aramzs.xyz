> The two computer scientists warned in their paper that “we believe the issue of advertising causes enough mixed incentives that it is crucial to have a competitive search engine that is transparent and in the academic realm,” but that wasn’t going to be Google. The partners left Stanford and, by way of a Menlo Park garage, landed in their Palo Alto office.
