> With an early professor recruit, the entomologist Vernon Kellogg, Jordan positioned the small department at the emerging frontier of life science. Though it didn’t last long under that name, bionomics, with its vision of “degenerate” races and outstandingly normal heroes, underpins Palo Alto’s ethos into the present day.
