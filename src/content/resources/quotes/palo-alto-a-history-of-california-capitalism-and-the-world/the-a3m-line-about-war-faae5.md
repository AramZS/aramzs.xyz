> The A3M line about the war becoming too costly, about capitalist imperialism only speaking one language, was precisely correct. Battered first in Vietnam by the Vietnamese people and second on the home front by the Third World solidarity movement, America’s leadership came to grips with the unthinkable: losing the war.
