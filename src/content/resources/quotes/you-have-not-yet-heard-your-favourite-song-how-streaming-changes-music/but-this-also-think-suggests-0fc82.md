> But this also, I think, suggests the nature of a post-Album-Era future that isn’t anti-album or anti-music, but pro-community. Another structural advantage of playlists over radio is that everybody can see all the songs in them at once, which in turn means they don’t have to rely on morbid repetition to achieve a shared experience.
