> Stern scrutiny revealed that basically any bluegrass instrumental with banjos was getting scored as if the banjo was singing. A quick scan confirmed that our initial training examples contained no bluegrass and quite possibly no banjo. Thus the ML process, encountering a banjo noise that wasn’t present in any of the training examples, matched it with something that was.
