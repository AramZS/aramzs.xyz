> I think, is that we should know. If we are personalizing all these individual experiences, I think the collective results should also be accessible to all participants. What artists did get algorithmically recommended on Discover Weekly this week? How many other people saw those particular running tights, and what did I not see because of those ads for an ointment I don’t need? Without this, there’s no good basis for trust.
