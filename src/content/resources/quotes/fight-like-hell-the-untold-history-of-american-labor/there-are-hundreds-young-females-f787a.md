> “There are hundreds of young females shipped from this State every year to the factory prison-houses, like cattle, sheep, and pigs sent to slaughter,” one Portland, Maine, newspaper lamented, as they were sent to labor “in the polluted and polluting manufacturing towns where they are prepared for a miserable life and a horrible death in the abodes of infamy.”
