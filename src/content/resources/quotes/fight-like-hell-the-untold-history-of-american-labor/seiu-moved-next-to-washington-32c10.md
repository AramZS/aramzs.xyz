> SEIU moved next to Washington, D.C., and Los Angeles, and the latter became a hot spot for more direct and confrontational action. In L.A. they used street theater and public protests to stir sympathy, and then they went straight to the sources of power. Building bosses started to see protestors lined up ready to chat at their favored restaurants and golf courses.
