> These things are being designed by people who have no idea what their professional ethical code is, or any recourse to deal with designers who break it. The work we do has become astonishingly complex in the last twenty years. I don’t just mean technically complex, that was the easy part. I mean ethically complex. Our field has matured and we need to mature along with it.
