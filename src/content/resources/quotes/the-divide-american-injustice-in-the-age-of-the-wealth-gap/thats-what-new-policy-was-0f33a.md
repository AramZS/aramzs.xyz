> that’s what the new policy was turning out to be: For the small subset of offenders who are guilty of truly systemic, transformative, organized crime, for offenders to whom losing a big case could be politically embarrassing, let’s carefully consider the collateral consequences of criminal prosecution. But for everybody else, let’s ignore them more than ever before.
