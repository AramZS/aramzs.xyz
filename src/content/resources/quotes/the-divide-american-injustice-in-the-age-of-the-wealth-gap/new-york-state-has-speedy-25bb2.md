> New York State has a speedy trial law, which mandates that the state must bring a misdemeanor case to trial within ninety days of arraignment. But in practice, the process gets stretched out almost indefinitely, thanks to an incredibly devious perversion of the system that the state has long used to bully misdemeanor defendants into pleas.
