> If student-athletes start removing the pedagogical mask and thinking of themselves as sellers of labor, they’re liable to start acting like it. Judge Wilken noted that, in the absence of the NCAA “cartel,” schools would undoubtedly offer the recruits for whom they compete most aggressively a share of the cash they generate.
