A small homage to the Mega-zine Channel 4 Teletext page by [[ Sara Joy's Website | Sara Joy ]]. 

Teletext holds a special place in my heart, I remember spending many an early morning browsing Teletext in the early 90s. I think I must have been maybe four or five and we had just got a new TV that not only was in colour, but also had Teletext support built in! Before then, I had only ever seen the technology on dedicated terminals at the Library.

I think I would like to make a similar homage myself.
