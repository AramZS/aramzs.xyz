- **Extract**: Copy your data off platforms you don't own.
- **Nullify**: Redirect or cross link to promote your own sites and platforms over big tech platforms. Leverage the power of big tech platforms for pushing people to your own web of sites.
- **Transform**: Turn the pale posts that were limited by the commercial and surveillance requirements of big tech platforms into something cool and new. 
- **Exchange**: Link to others, copyleft, and give away content to others who re-share and match your philosophy. Get permission to use their work and give permission to use yours. 
- **Remix**: Combine your work and the work of others to build a better, more interesting, and more fun web.
