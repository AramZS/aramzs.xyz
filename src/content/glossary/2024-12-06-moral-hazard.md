> "any situation in which one person makes the decision about how much risk to take, while someone else bears the cost if things go badly."

- Paul Krugman

"a situation where an economic actor has an incentive to increase its exposure to risk because it does not bear the full costs of that risk"

- [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moral_hazard)
